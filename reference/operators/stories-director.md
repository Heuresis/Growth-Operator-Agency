## Core IP — 7-Day Story Sequence

Weekly IG Story sequence with rotating frameworks. **Never repeat same framework 2 days in a row.**

| Day | Framework | Weight | Purpose |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Proof From Many Clients + Not-Luck | Orange / heavy | Authority density |
| 2 | Clarity About Program (FAQ) | Grey / light | Reduce friction |
| 3 | Break Big Objection / Limiting Belief | Orange / heavy | Objection killing |
| 4 | Educative + Differentiation (how we solve) | Orange / heavy | Mechanism teaching |
| 5 | Personal Connection | Grey / light | Humanize |
| 6 | Proof + Skepticism Removal | Orange / heavy | Fresh case study |
| 7 | Mission | Grey / light | Bigger purpose |

**Weighted balance: 4 orange + 3 grey.** Prevents over-sales feel.

## The 4-Layer Story Anatomy

Every story has 4 layers simultaneously:

1. **Text** (script engine) — the message/claim
2. **Image** (background) — **NEVER blank** — subconscious proof or connection
3. **Proof-Screenshots** (trust) — Stripe, testimonials, DMs (proof, not claims)
4. **Visuals** (authority) — charts/graphs/Miro/diagrams

Invariants:
- Never blank background
- Never claim without screenshot
- Mix orange + grey weekly
- Each story sells the next (momentum)

## Day 4 Educational Sub-Templates (3 ideas)

- Big picture + solution (overview frame)
- Big reason + unique model (why-it-works frame)
- Focus on one pain + unique mechanism (how-we-solve frame)

## Day 6 Proof Sub-Templates (4 formats)

- Simple storytelling (arc)
- A→B storytelling (transformation)
- Testimonial-first (video or screenshot)
- Objection-first (counter assumed skepticism)
