## Canonical Frameworks

### 1. "Death by Papercuts" Core Thesis

> *"Scaling an info product isn't difficult by any means, but it DOES require lots of attention to detail. Little 'leaks' throughout your customer journey = death by a million papercuts. Be extremely detail-oriented with everything before ever spending a dollar on paid ads."*

A well-designed offer loses 60-80% of potential revenue to tiny operational leaks. Each leak alone is invisible. Combined, they kill the business.

### 2. 8-Stage Customer Journey Audit

Weekly operational audit across all 8 stages:

1. **Traffic** — CPM, CPC, CTR, frequency, lookalike quality
2. **Landing Page** — page speed, device split, opt-in copy, form friction
3. **Application** — start rate, completion rate, qualification logic
4. **TY / Confirmation Page** — show-up prep, video engagement, expectation-set
5. **Setter** — response time < 5 min, show-up rate, qualification accuracy
6. **Pre-Call Nurture** — bullet-point prep delivered, 24h/6h/1h reminder cadence
7. **On-Call** — discovery quality, pitch execution, close rate, objection handling
8. **Post-Purchase** — payment processing, onboarding speed, first-win delivery

Every stage has 3-7 operational levers.

### 3. 7-Step Launch Process

Not a creative launch — the operational sequence for launching/scaling an offer:
1. **Initial offering** — MVP, 5-10 customers, no paid traffic
2. **Beta launch** — 30-50 customers, case-study capture, objection mining
3. **Reposition + Infra** — tighten positioning + build tech stack
4. **Hard launch** — full paid traffic, email list + partners
5. **Second wind / Live event** — mid-cycle re-acceleration
6. **Optimization** — week-by-week leak plugging
7. **Amplify + New conversion mechanisms** — once funnel #1 dialed, add #2

**Rule:** don't add conversion mechanism #2 until #1 has proven leak-free at $500K+/mo.

### 4. 60/30/10 Revenue Mix (ideal at $130K+/mo)

- **60% call funnel revenue** (highest intent, highest trust, largest AOV)
- **30% webinar funnel revenue** (scaleable, evergreen or live)
- **10% email broadcast revenue** (low-cost, high-margin reactivation)

Balances acquisition cost, scale ceiling, and revenue quality. If any single channel > 80%, business is fragile.

### 5. Operational Rules

**Rule 1 — "Always start HIGH TICKET"**
Low-ticket is an ascension mechanism, not a standalone business. Prove high-ticket first.

**Rule 2 — "Setter-to-closer model (staff depending)"**
- <$100K/mo: founder-closes + admin setter
- $100K-$500K: 1 closer + 2 setters
- $500K-$1M: 2 closers + 4 setters + 1 SDR
- $1M+: full sales team

**Rule 3 — "Don't try 3 sports at once"**
Master ONE at any given cycle (webinar scaling / VSL scaling / DM-to-call). Master it. Add next only after first is on rails.

### 6. KPI-Driven Diagnostics

> *"Complaining about performance but not understanding why you're underperforming is the same as complaining about being out of shape but never tracking a calorie or a workout."*

Core metrics dashboard:
- **Acquisition:** CPL, CAC, lead→app, app→show, show→close
- **Economics:** AOV, LTV, LTV:CAC (≥3:1), gross margin, payback period
- **Ops:** Setter response time <5min, closer utilization, refund rate <5%, show-rate >60% for apps
- **Delivery:** First-win time, NPS, retention, upsell rate
- **Team:** Revenue per employee, cost per seat, utilization

### 7. Implement → Iterate → Amplify

> *"The entire game is: Implement, Iterate, Amplify. Rinse and repeat."*

Most businesses get stuck in Implement (change for change's sake) or jump to Amplify too early (scale broken economics). **Iterate is where the money lives.**
