## Canonical programs

### 1. Agency Navigator / Agency Accelerator — the canonical SMMA playbook
Evolution: Agency Incubator → Agency Navigator → Agency Accelerator. Canonical structure (from public sales pages + reviews):
1. **Niche selection** — pick ONE service vertical.
2. **Offer** — retainer-priced done-for-you service (usually Meta ads).
3. **Outreach** — cold email, DM, and loom video at volume.
4. **Sales** — discovery call → audit → close.
5. **Fulfillment** — hire/freelance stack; operator stays in sales.
6. **Scale** — systematize and hire account managers.

### 2. Digital Launchpad (2023 → current)
Subscription-first e-learning portal. Progressive unlock model:
- **Month 4** — live monthly calls with Iman.
- **Month 6** — private chat channels with millionaire mentors.
- **Month 12** — advanced content, events, premium tiers.
- **Month 24** — "Emperor" status.
Catalog beyond Iman includes 10+ business-model courses: copywriting, web design, affiliate, e-commerce, media buying, personal branding, trading, etc.

### 3. Educate (the umbrella brand)
Positioning: *"World-class learning rooted in experience and real-life application at a fraction of the cost of traditional academic institutions."* Platform for 13+ courses from vetted operators — Iman productized not just his own IP but a *course-distribution network*.

## Canonical frameworks embedded across programs
- **SMMA playbook** (Niche → Offer → Outreach → Sales → Fulfillment → Scale) — the original the agency director 6-stage model.
- **Retainer-priced ad management** as default agency offer (de-risks client, compounds revenue).
- **Loom-audit outreach** — personalized pre-sell at cold-outbound scale.
- **Progressive-unlock subscription** — retention mechanic where months of tenure gate the highest-value rooms.
- **Course-as-product-network** — curate operators instead of being the only teacher.

## Invariants
- Pick one niche, one service — do not generalize at start.
- Sell retainers, not projects.
- Outreach is a numbers game with personalization gates (Loom audit).
- Founder stays in sales until $30K–$50K/mo MRR.
- Progressive-unlock > lifetime-access for subscription retention.
- Distribute other operators' IP to expand the moat beyond yourself.
