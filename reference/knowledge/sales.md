# Sales — Shared Knowledge

## Purpose
Shared vocabulary, funnel archetypes, VSL variants, and conversion mechanics inherited by every skill in the Sales department. Sales turns the foundations-ready offer into a revenue-producing machine — VSLs that sell, funnels that convert, and crossroads closes that move prospects off the fence.

## Department Skills (2)
- `/build-vsl` — author a Video Sales Letter in one of 5 canonical variants
- `/build-funnel` — architect a conversion funnel from 7 archetypes

## Core Vocabulary
- **VSL** — Video Sales Letter. Long-form conversion video (7–60 min) that walks a cold viewer to a buying decision.
- **Crossroads Close** — The moment the VSL forces a binary: keep doing what you are doing (and keep losing) or choose this path. Placed at 65–75% mark.
- **Mechanism-Threading** — Every section of the VSL references the unique mechanism by name ≥ 1 time. 12+ threads across a 20-min VSL.
- **Sacred-Format 3/3** — Three proof blocks, three stakes-raises, three belief-dissolutions, spread evenly across the script.
- **Hook Density** — Hook every 90s for the first 10 min, every 2 min after. Missed hook = scroll.
- **Offer Stack** — The itemized list of components with ascribed dollar value; stack total must be ≥ 3× price.
- **CVR** — Conversion Rate. Cold → Lead: ≥ 25%. Lead → Call: ≥ 15%. Call → Close: ≥ 20%. VSL → Buy (direct): ≥ 2%.
- **AOV / EPC** — Average Order Value / Earnings Per Click. EPC ≥ $1.50 = scalable funnel.
- **Show-Rate** — % booked calls that show. Target ≥ 65% (see nurture.md).

## 7 Funnel Archetypes
1. **Opt-in → VSL → Call** (high-ticket, $3K+). Cold to booked-call in one session.
2. **Opt-in → VSL → Direct Buy** (mid-ticket, $100–$997). No call. VSL must do 100% of selling.
3. **Lead-Magnet → Nurture → Webinar → Call** (premium, $5K–$25K). 7–14 day ramp.
4. **Application Funnel** (elite, $10K+). Qualify before giving price. VSL + written application + sales call.
5. **Tripwire → Core → Upsell** (e-com/info, $7 → $97 → $497). Ascension through value ladder.
6. **Free Trial → Paid** (SaaS/membership). 7–14 day trial with activation nudges.
7. **Challenge Funnel** (5-day/7-day/30-day challenge → offer). Commitment + consistency. Close on final day.

Match funnel to offer price: < $200 direct buy; $200–$2K webinar/VSL; $2K+ call or application.

## 5 VSL Variants
1. **Problem-Agitate-Solve (PAS)** — Classic. Name pain, agitate consequences, reveal solution. Best for: broad pain, cold traffic.
2. **Story-Driven** — Origin story → collapse → breakthrough → mechanism. Best for: founder-led brand, warm audience.
3. **Mechanism-First** — Lead with the unique mechanism as a "discovery". Best for: technical/scientific-sounding offers.
4. **Contrast/Enemy** — "They do X, we do Y, here's why theirs fails". Best for: crowded categories.
5. **Case-Study Stack** — Multiple before/after proofs carry the pitch. Best for: results-heavy niches (fitness, biz-opp).

## Crossroads Close — The Canonical Structure
Placed at ~70% of runtime. Three-beat sequence:
1. **Stakes** — "If you keep doing what you are doing, here is where you will be in 12 months."
2. **The Fork** — "There are two paths: the one you are on, and this one."
3. **The Invitation** — "Click below. Book the call. We will talk. No pressure. Only clarity."

Followed by: price reveal, stack recap, guarantee, scarcity, objection-handling (FAQ), final CTA.

## Mechanism-Threading Checklist
- [ ] Hook references mechanism by name
- [ ] First proof block uses mechanism as explanation
- [ ] Crossroads close names the mechanism
- [ ] Stack items trace back to mechanism
- [ ] FAQ section re-anchors mechanism
- [ ] Guarantee rides on mechanism integrity
- Minimum 12 threads in a 20-min VSL. Count them. Literally count them.

## Sacred-Format 3/3 Rule
Every VSL must contain:
- **3 proof blocks** — data / testimonial / demo. One each, spread evenly.
- **3 stakes-raises** — reminders of the cost of inaction. Positions 25%, 50%, 75%.
- **3 belief-dissolutions** — one for each arm of the Limiting Belief Triad.

If any bucket has < 3 elements, the VSL is not shippable.

## Handoff Patterns
1. Foundations ships `offer.md` (Value Equation ≥ 150) → `/build-vsl` consumes it. **Gate:** offer must name mechanism + limiting belief.
2. `/build-vsl` produces `vsl-script.md` — Sacred-Format 3/3 passed, mechanism-threading verified. **Gate:** 12+ threads, 3/3 blocks present, crossroads at 65–75%.
3. `/build-funnel` consumes VSL + offer → produces `funnel-map.md` with pages, emails, automations. **Gate:** matches archetype to offer price band.
4. Funnel Live → marketing department traffic turns on (ad-creative, story-sequence). Nurture takes over post-optin.

## Anti-patterns (block these)
- VSL with < 12 mechanism threads (audience never retains the mechanism)
- Crossroads placed before 50% (too soon, stakes not earned) or after 85% (viewers gone)
- Offer stack total < 3× price (perceived value fail)
- Funnel archetype mismatch (high-ticket direct-buy, low-ticket application)
- VSL written in generic "you-speak" without founder voice (voice fidelity < 8/10)
- No guarantee (doubles objection load)
