## 10. Voice Enforcement Protocol

### 10.1 Pre-ship checks (every asset)

Before any customer-facing asset ships, it must pass:

- [ ] Banned-vocabulary scan clean (global + creator-specific)

- [ ] No phrases_to_avoid present (Section 6)
- [ ] Tone matches context per Section 2.3
- [ ] Sentence length distribution roughly matches Section 4.1
- [ ] Punctuation habits honored per Section 4.5

For sacred-format assets (Offer Doc, VSL, Webinar Script, Launch Plan, Case Study, Positioning, Sales Page):
