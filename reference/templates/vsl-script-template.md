# VSL Script — 15-Step Template

> **Source skill:** `/build-vsl`
> **Output location:** `output/build-vsl/{YYYY-MM-DD}-vsl-15-step.md`
> **Structure:** 15-step VSL methodology
> **Runtime target:** 25-45 minutes spoken length
> **Gate:** all 15 steps present, 8 required beliefs mapped, mechanism named in step 3, stack + guarantee in step 12, banned-vocab clean.

Replace every `[FILL]` with real content pulled from the Offer Document, ICP Document, Positioning Document, and Brand Voice Document. Do not ship with brackets remaining.

## Step 1 — Pattern Interrupt Hook (0:00-0:15)

**Objective:** Stop the scroll. Earn the next 15 seconds.

**Hook type (pick one):**
- Curiosity-gap statement
- Contrarian claim
- Big number
- Identity call-out
- Visual surprise

**Script:**
> [FILL — 20-40 words. Must include one of: specific number, named entity, emotional word, pattern-break. Not a question. Not "Hey everyone." Not "Let me tell you about...".]

**Visual cue:** [FILL — what's on screen during this line]

**Belief installed:** Belief 1 (Reproduction) + Belief 5 (This-offer) seed

---

## Step 2 — Promise + Stakes (0:15-0:45)

**Objective:** Name exactly what the viewer walks away with if they watch — and what it costs them to skip.

**Script:**
> In the next [N] minutes I'm going to show you [FILL — specific outcome]. By the end you'll [FILL — what they can do differently]. If you close this video now, you'll [FILL — the cost of not watching: not financial; the loss of insight].

**Voice-of-customer language requirement:** Use at least 1 verbatim phrase from ICP Section 9.

---

## Step 3 — Name the Mechanism (0:45-1:30)

**Objective:** Plant the named mechanism in the viewer's head in the first 90 seconds.

**Script:**
> The reason most [ICP avatar] can't [outcome] isn't [common blame] — it's something called [FILL — mechanism name from Positioning Doc]. [FILL — 2-3 sentences explaining the mechanism at a grade-6 level.] Once you see this, you can't un-see it.

**Belief installed:** Belief 5 (This-offer) + Belief 6 (Better/faster)

---

## Step 4 — Credentials / Who Am I (1:30-3:00)

**Objective:** Install Belief 7 (Trust-person) without sounding like a CV read.

**Script pattern:**
> My name is [FILL]. Over the last [N years] I've [FILL — specific accomplishment]. I've worked with [FILL — archetype of buyers, count]. Collectively they've [FILL — aggregate result, archetype-anonymized].
>
> But that's not why you should listen to me. The reason you should listen is [FILL — the specific thing about your vantage point that makes you uniquely qualified to teach this mechanism].

**Voice rule:** Brand Voice Document authority posture must govern — peer / coach / mentor / category-king.

---

## Step 5 — The Origin Problem (3:00-5:00)

**Objective:** Show the viewer you've been where they are. Install derivative-desire and trust.

**Script pattern:**
> [N years ago] I was [FILL — specific earlier state, matching ICP Layer 2-3 pain]. I tried [FILL — conventional approach] and it [failed how]. I was [FILL — emotional state, matching ICP psychographics].

**Voice-of-customer:** Echo at least 1 ICP Layer 3 (private) pain quote verbatim.

---

## Step 6 — The Discovery (5:00-7:00)

**Objective:** The turning-point story beat. The moment the mechanism revealed itself.

**Script:**
> Then one day [FILL — specific moment]. I realized [FILL — the mechanism insight]. I tested it with [FILL — first test]. Within [N weeks/months] [FILL — specific outcome].

**Belief installed:** Belief 3 (Can-fulfill) seed.

---

## Step 7 — Proof / Results Montage (7:00-9:00)

**Objective:** Show the mechanism works for more than just you. Install Belief 8 (Trust-expertise).

**Script pattern:**
> I started teaching this to [FILL — archetype]. Here's what happened:
>
> - [Archetype A]: [outcome + timeframe]
> - [Archetype B]: [outcome + timeframe]
> - [Archetype C]: [outcome + timeframe]
>
> These aren't cherry-picked. Across [N] people who applied [mechanism], [X%] hit [benchmark].

**Rule:** Every result claim must be real. No fabrication. If anonymized, note the archetype and keep outcome specific.

---

## Step 8 — The Problem Redefined (9:00-11:30)

**Objective:** Reframe the viewer's stuck-point using the mechanism lens. Install Belief 5 (This-offer).

**Script:**
> Here's what almost everyone gets wrong about [problem]: [FILL — the common misdiagnosis]. The real issue isn't [common blame]. It's [FILL — the mechanism's diagnosis].
>
> Let me show you why. [FILL — 3-5 sentence explanation with an analogy or micro-demo.]

---

## Step 9 — The 3 Secrets / Principles (11:30-17:00)

**Objective:** Teach enough to convince them the mechanism is real, without giving the whole system.

**Script template per secret:**

### Secret 1 — [FILL]
> Most [ICP archetype] believe [common belief]. The truth is [contrarian reframe]. Here's what this means practically: [FILL — 2-3 sentence application]. [FILL — one micro-example].

### Secret 2 — [FILL]
> [Same pattern]

### Secret 3 — [FILL]
> [Same pattern]

**Voice rule:** Use contrarian beliefs from Positioning Doc Section 3.2 as the spine of each secret.

---

## Step 10 — The Transition (17:00-18:30)

**Objective:** Move from teaching to offering.

**Script:**
> So now you know [recap the 3 secrets in one sentence]. The question is — can you do this on your own? Maybe. It took me [N years]. Most [archetype] never put the pieces together because [FILL — a real barrier].
>
> What if I told you there's a faster way?

**Belief installed:** Belief 4 (Urgency) seed.

---

## Step 11 — Offer Reveal (18:30-21:00)

**Objective:** Name the offer. Present the mechanism → offer bridge.

**Script:**
> I built [Offer Name] for [ICP archetype] who want [outcome]. It's a [delivery format] that walks you through the entire [mechanism] step-by-step, starting with [FILL — first milestone].
>
> Here's what's inside.

**Visual cue:** Stack slide appears.

---

## Step 12 — Stack Slide (21:00-26:00)

**Objective:** Itemized value stack + bonuses + pricing + guarantee on one visual and in verbal sequence.

### Script outline

**Core components:**
> When you join [Offer Name], here's what you get:
>
> - [Component 1] — [one line] — worth $[N] on its own
> - [Component 2] — [one line] — worth $[N]
> - [Component 3] — [one line] — worth $[N]
> - [Component 4] — [one line] — worth $[N]
> - [Component 5] — [one line] — worth $[N]

**Total stated value so far:** $[SUM]

**Bonus stack (2nd-best first, best last):**
> And because I want to remove every possible reason you'd hesitate, I'm adding:
>
> - **Bonus 1** — [name] — [one line] — to solve [objection] — worth $[N]
> - **Bonus 2** — [name] — [one line] — to solve [objection] — worth $[N]
> - **Bonus 3** — [name] — [one line] — to solve [objection] — worth $[N]
> - **Bonus 4** — [name] — [one line] — to solve [objection] — worth $[N]
> - **Bonus 5** — [name] — [one line] — to solve [objection] — worth $[N] (this one is the climax)

**Total stack value:** $[SUM]

### Price reveal

**Script:**
> If you tried to build this on your own, it would cost you [anchor price]. If you hired someone to do it, [higher anchor]. But for the next [urgency window], your investment is not $[anchor]. Not $[mid-anchor]. Your investment is $[primary price].

---

## Step 13 — Guarantee (26:00-28:00)

**Objective:** Remove risk. Install Belief 7 (Trust-person) fully.

**Script:**
> Here's my promise to you. [FILL — guarantee copy from Offer Doc Section 8.5]. That means the only way you lose is if you don't [FILL — reasonable condition].

**Visual cue:** Guarantee badge.

---

## Step 14 — Urgency / Scarcity (28:00-30:00)

**Objective:** Install Belief 4 (Urgency) with real scarcity, not fabricated.

**Real scarcity options:**
- Cohort start date (real)
- Seat count (real capacity)
- Bonus time-limit (real expiry)
- Price increase (real, scheduled)

**Script pattern:**
> This isn't a fake countdown. [FILL — specific real scarcity mechanism]. After that [FILL — what changes].

**NEVER** invent scarcity. If there's no real scarcity, skip this step — don't fake one.

---

## Step 15 — Call to Action + Close (30:00-end)

**Objective:** Name the next step. Remove friction. Close.

**Script pattern:**
> Here's what to do next. Click the button below this video. You'll see [FILL — next page description]. Fill in [FILL — the next action], and [FILL — what happens next].
>
> If you're not ready, that's fine. Watch this again. Think about it. But don't let another [N months] pass feeling [FILL — ICP Layer 3 pain echo].
>
> You know what the [mechanism] is now. The only question is whether you want to do it alone or with me. I'll see you inside.

**Final frame:** CTA button + URL + urgency reinforcement.
