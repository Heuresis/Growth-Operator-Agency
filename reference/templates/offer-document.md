# Offer Document — Template

> **Source skill:** `/design-offer`
> **Output location:** `output/design-offer/{YYYY-MM-DD}-offer-document.md`
> **Sections:** 12 (all required for ship)
> **Gate:** Value Equation Score >= 150, LTV:CAC >= 3:1, mechanism named, 5+ bonuses each countering an ICP objection, ROI-positive guarantee, limiting-belief-aligned transformation.

Replace every `[FILL]` with real content pulled from the ICP Document, Positioning Document, and `company.yaml` compartments 1+3. Do not ship with brackets remaining.
