## 6. Phase 4 — Cart Close (final 24-72 hours)

### 6.1 Objective

Maximum urgency. Close undecided buyers. Respect the deadline.

### 6.2 Activities

| Time before close | Action | Owner |
|---|---|---|
| T-24h | "24 hours" email + social posts | Email + Content |
| T-12h | FAQ email + SMS blast | Email + SMS |
| T-6h | "Last chance" email + live "cart closing" social | Email + Creator |
| T-4h | Creator personal note email | Creator |
| T-1h | Final 1-hour email | Email |
| T-30min | Social media reminders | Creator / Content |
| T-0 (cart close) | Cart closes (IMMUTABLE) | Ops |
| T+1h | "Closed" email + waitlist info | Email |

### 6.3 Cart-close rules (non-negotiable)

- **No extensions.** Under no condition. "24 more hours because so many asked" destroys list trust permanently.
- **If technical issue occurs:** fix within 2 hours max; if not fixable, cart stays closed and buyers who reported the issue before close are manually enrolled.
- **Sales team stops soft-closing in last 2 hours** — only hard close.

### 6.4 KPIs

- % of total enrollment in final 48 hours: typically 30-50%
- % of total enrollment in final 12 hours: typically 15-25%
- Cart-close cohort AOV: [target vs launch average]

## 7. Phase 5 — Post-Launch + Debrief (T+1 to T+14)

### 7.1 Objective

Onboard buyers. Reactivate the unsold. Document learnings. Feed next launch.

### 7.2 Onboarding sequence (buyers)

Cross-reference `email-sequence-template.md` Type 7 (Post-Purchase Onboarding).

| Day | Touch | Owner |
|---|---|---|
| T+0 | Welcome email + community invite | Automation |
| T+1 | First-win prompt | Automation |
| T+3 | Live onboarding call (if cohort) | Creator / CSM |
| T+7 | First-milestone check | CSM |
| T+14 | Activation check + support call offer | CSM |

### 7.3 Unsold-list re-engagement

- T+3: "Waitlist" email with next-cohort window
- T+7: Soft-touch content continuation
- T+30: Possible rolling-cart or payment-plan offer

### 7.4 Debrief (T+7 to T+14)

Cross-reference `post-launch-report.md`.

- Survey buyers: why they bought, what almost stopped them, what they expect
- Survey non-buyers: why they didn't, what would have moved them, interest in next cohort
- Run aggregate KPI analysis
- Document failure modes (asset drift, cart-close issues, tech glitches)
- Update `company.yaml` compartments with lessons learned
- Feed the next launch cycle

### 7.5 Team retrospective

- 60-min meeting, all launch hands
- Agenda: what worked / what broke / what we won't repeat / what we'll try next time
- Written debrief published in team wiki within T+14
