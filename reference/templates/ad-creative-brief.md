## 7. Ad-Type Specific Briefs

### 7.1 Profile Funnel Ad

- Objective: grow creator's owned audience + prime for organic content
- Hook: identity call-out or personal story open
- Body: creator-as-person, relate + teach + invite
- CTA: Follow / subscribe / join newsletter
- Visual: direct-to-camera, authentic setting

### 7.2 Retargeting Ad

- Objective: move warm prospect from consideration to conversion
- Hook: reference prior touchpoint ("If you watched my VSL...")
- Body: objection handling + new proof + offer reinforcement
- CTA: Book / buy / enroll
- Visual: can be more polished; warm audience tolerates production

### 7.3 Video Hook Ad

- Objective: maximize scroll-stop rate + view-through to minimum 15s
- Hook: pattern interrupt + curiosity-gap claim
- Body: teach one mechanism component fast
- CTA: Watch full video / visit landing page
- Visual: high-motion, strong first frame, kinetic text

### 7.4 Testimonial Ad

- Objective: borrow credibility from buyer voices
- Hook: start with buyer's strongest quote
- Body: buyer story — before / shift / after in their voice
- CTA: Apply / enroll / learn more
- Visual: buyer speaking to camera, lower-thirds with their archetype descriptor
- Rules: real people, signed release, no fabrication, archetype-anonymize only with permission

### 7.5 UGC-Style Ad

- Objective: look native to feed, not like an ad
- Hook: authentic first-person reaction
- Body: mobile-shot, handheld, informal
- CTA: native ("link in bio" / swipe up)
- Visual: vertical, low-production, high-authenticity

### 7.6 Carousel Ad

- Objective: teach or compare across multiple cards
- Card 1: Hook
- Cards 2-N: Teaching points or comparison
- Final card: CTA
- Visual: consistent template, strong typography, single-point-per-card

### 7.7 Static Image Ad

- Objective: lowest-production awareness
- Composition: hook text + image that supports + CTA
- Color: high-contrast, on-brand
- Text: 20% rule for META (keep text <= 20% of image area historically; rules change — verify current spec)

### 7.8 Long-Form Story Ad

- Objective: warm-audience conversion on complex offer
- Format: 2-5 min video or 400+ word text post
- Structure: problem-agitate-solve + mechanism + offer + guarantee
- CTA: direct to funnel
- Visual: conversational, educational tone
