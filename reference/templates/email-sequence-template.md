## Global rules (every email)

1. **One CTA** per email. Every link should point to the same destination. No decision fatigue.
2. **Subject line A/B pair** — never ship with one subject line.
3. **Preview text** — 40-90 chars, extends the subject (doesn't duplicate).
4. **Voice** — Brand Voice Document governs. Minimum 1 phrase from phrases_to_use per email.
5. **Length** — 150-400 words for nurture/launch, 80-200 for cart-close, 200-500 for indoctrination.
6. **PS line** — always include; it's the second-most-read element after the subject.
7. **Send time** — test but default to the creator's list's proven time.
8. **Banned-vocab scan** — mandatory before ship.

## Sequence Templates (all 8 types)

### Type 1 — Indoctrination Sequence (5 emails, daily)

**Email 1 — Welcome + expectation-setting**
- Subject A: [FILL]
- Body: Welcome, what to expect, creator's unique angle, promise, community link
- CTA: Follow creator / Join community

**Email 2 — Origin story**
- Subject A: [FILL — curiosity hook about creator's past]
- Body: Before state → turning point → after state → why this matters for reader
- CTA: Read mechanism primer (next email tease)

**Email 3 — Mechanism primer**
- Subject A: [FILL — curiosity + specificity]
- Body: The mechanism named + why it exists + who it's for + one micro-example
- CTA: Apply first principle today

**Email 4 — First-win prompt**
- Subject A: [FILL — action-oriented]
- Body: Specific small action reader can take in 10 min, artifact they produce, reply-ask
- CTA: Reply with your result

**Email 5 — Community + soft offer mention**
- Subject A: [FILL]
- Body: Community invite, soft mention of core offer for when reader is ready, next-step
- CTA: Join community OR reply with a question

### Type 3 — Nurture Sequence (ongoing, weekly)

**Email kind rotation:** Story / Teach / Case / Pattern / Question / Behind-scenes / Contrarian

**Email template (per send):**
- Subject A: [FILL]
- Opening: hook (story / stat / claim)
- Middle: one idea developed in 2-4 short paragraphs
- Close: one-line takeaway + question OR soft CTA
- PS: reference next send or surface offer mention

**Objective:** keep list warm. Build voice recognition. Surface objections. Plant offer seeds without burning.

## Appendix A — Subject Line Patterns

### Curiosity
- "The [specific thing] about [topic]"
- "Why I stopped [common behavior]"
- "[Specific number] [thing] that [result]"

### Specificity
- "[N] things [ICP archetype] do that [outcome]"
- "How [archetype] went from [X] to [Y] in [time]"

### Urgency
- "[Deadline descriptor]"
- "Last chance: [specific]"
- "[N hours] remaining"

### Question
- "Are you [specific state]?"
- "Did you see [specific]?"

### Contrarian
- "Everyone is wrong about [topic]"
- "Stop doing [common advice]"

---

## Appendix B — Preview Text Patterns

- Extends subject: adds specificity ("...and here's what I learned")
- Sets stakes: "Open before [time]"
- Personal hook: "Had to share this..."
- Curiosity close: "...but there's a twist"

---

## Appendix C — PS Patterns

- Proof-stack: "PS — [client archetype] just [outcome]. Here's how."
- Urgency reinforcement: "PS — cart closes at [time]. No extensions."
- Next-email tease: "PS — tomorrow I'm sharing [specific]. Don't miss it."
- Reply-ask: "PS — reply with a number 1-5 on where you're at. I read every one."
