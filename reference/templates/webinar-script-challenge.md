# Webinar Script — Multi-Day Challenge Archetype

> **Source skill:** `/webinar-script` (challenge variant)
> **Output location:** `output/webinar-script/{YYYY-MM-DD}-challenge-{N}-day.md`
> **Structure:** 3-Day / 5-Day / 7-Day challenge — one session per day
> **Runtime target:** 60-90 min per day
> **Gate:** each day delivers a micro-win, cart opens Day N-1 or Day N, community engagement mechanism defined, post-cart replay plan scheduled.

Replace every `[FILL]` with real content. Do not ship with brackets remaining.

## Structure Overview

### 5-Day Challenge (default)

| Day | Theme | Micro-win | Pitch pressure |
|---|---|---|---|
| Day 1 | Mindset / framing | Clarity on the real problem | None |
| Day 2 | Discovery / audit | Personal diagnosis | None |
| Day 3 | Core teach | First working output | Soft pitch opens |
| Day 4 | Proof + cases | Committed identity shift | Medium pitch |
| Day 5 | Pitch + close | Enrolled or not | Full pitch, deadline, CTA |

### 3-Day Variant

| Day | Theme | Micro-win | Pitch pressure |
|---|---|---|---|
| Day 1 | Teach + diagnosis | First framework applied | None |
| Day 2 | Apply + cases | Committed shift | Soft pitch |
| Day 3 | Close + CTA | Enrolled or not | Full pitch |

### 7-Day Variant

| Day | Theme | Micro-win |
|---|---|---|
| Day 1 | Framing | Clarity |
| Day 2 | Diagnosis | Personal audit |
| Day 3 | Pillar 1 | First move |
| Day 4 | Pillar 2 | Second move |
| Day 5 | Pillar 3 | Third move |
| Day 6 | Synthesis + cases | Full picture |
| Day 7 | Pitch + close | Enrolled |

## Day 1 Script

### Objective

Set the container. Teach the diagnostic frame. Engineer a first micro-win that leaves attendees thinking "this is different."

### Opening (0:00-10:00)

**Welcome + container-setting:**
> Welcome to [Challenge name]. Over the next [N] days we're going to [FILL — outcome]. Here's what to expect: [FILL — daily format, commitment, community]. Today, Day 1, we're covering [FILL — day 1 theme]. By the end of today you'll [micro-win].

**Ground rules:**
- Show up live if you can
- Replay is available [duration]
- Community is where the real conversations happen
- Do the work — don't just watch

### Core teach (10:00-45:00)

**Framework:** [FILL — day 1 framework, usually the diagnostic frame]

**Teaching structure:**
- Concept explanation (5 min)
- Diagram / visual (2 min)
- Worked example (5 min)
- Application prompt (3 min)
- Breakout / chat engagement (5 min)
- Synthesis (5 min)

**Voice-of-customer plant:** Echo 2-3 ICP verbatim pain phrases during teaching. Attendees should hear their own words.

### Daily action (45:00-55:00)

**Assignment:**
> Before tomorrow's session, do this one thing: [FILL — specific action that produces a tangible artifact].
>
> Post your work in the community. I'll review the top submissions tomorrow.

### Close (55:00-60:00)

**Recap + tomorrow tease:**
> Today you [micro-win accomplished]. Tomorrow we're covering [Day 2 theme] and you'll [Day 2 micro-win]. See you then.

### Community engagement beat

- Post the daily prompt in the community within 30 min
- Respond to the first 10-20 posts personally
- Highlight 3-5 best in tomorrow's opening

## Day 2 Script

### Objective

Deepen the diagnostic. Surface every attendee's personal version of the problem. Soft-seed the mechanism without naming the offer.

### Opening (0:00-10:00)

**Recap:**
> Yesterday we covered [Day 1 theme]. I reviewed your submissions in the community — huge insights. [FILL — highlight 3-5 specific examples by first-name or handle.]

**Today's promise:**
> Today, Day 2, we're covering [Day 2 theme]. By the end you'll [micro-win].

### Core teach (10:00-45:00)

[FILL — Day 2 framework]

**Structure:** same as Day 1 — concept + diagram + example + prompt + synthesis.

**Voice plant:** 2-3 more verbatim ICP phrases.

### Daily action (45:00-55:00)

> Your assignment: [FILL]. Post in community.

### Close (55:00-60:00)

**Tomorrow tease:**
> Tomorrow is the core day. We'll cover [Day 3 theme] and I'll share [specific asset or framework]. Also — heads up — I'll be talking about what happens after this challenge ends for those of you who want to keep going. No pressure, just transparency.

**Pitch-pressure:** Soft seed. Announce the upcoming pitch honestly.

## Day 3 Script (the pivot day — or the close day for 3-day variant)

### Objective (for 5-day and 7-day)

Deliver the biggest teach of the challenge. Engineer the largest micro-win so far. Open the soft pitch window.

### Objective (for 3-day)

Close. Full pitch. Deadline.

### Opening (0:00-10:00)

**Recap + momentum:**
> Halfway through. Look at what you've already done: [FILL — recap of days 1-2 outcomes]. Today is the biggest teach of the challenge.

### Core teach (10:00-45:00)

[FILL — Day 3 framework, usually the core mechanism]

### Soft pitch opens (for 5-day / 7-day)

**Script:**
> Before we move on, I want to be transparent. What you're learning here is the outer shell of a system I teach inside [Offer Name]. I don't say that to hook you — I say it so you know what you're getting. Later in the challenge I'll share more about it. For now, keep applying what we're covering.

### Daily action (45:00-55:00)

**Script:**
> [FILL — assignment that starts to require the depth of the full system]

### Close (55:00-60:00)

**For 3-day challenge:** bridge directly to Day 3 full pitch section below.

**For 5-day / 7-day:** tease tomorrow.

## Day 4 Script (5-day) / Days 4-6 Script (7-day)

### Objective

Stack proof. Show the mechanism working across archetypes. Escalate pitch pressure gently.

### Opening (0:00-10:00)

**Proof open:**
> Yesterday I asked you to [Day 3 assignment]. Here's what I saw [FILL — highlight submissions + acknowledge by name].

### Core teach (10:00-35:00)

[FILL — Day 4/5/6 framework, typically proof-heavy]

### Proof stack (35:00-55:00)

Walk through 3-5 case archetypes in detail. Each case:
- Starting state (archetype-anonymized)
- Specific mechanism steps applied
- Specific outcome + timeframe
- Key insight the attendee can replicate

### Medium pitch (55:00-60:00)

**Script:**
> Tomorrow is Day [final]. Tomorrow I'm going to share [Offer Name] — the full system. If you're ahead of the curve and already know you want in, [URL] has the details now. For everyone else, tomorrow.

## Day 5 Script (final day — the close)

### Objective

Full pitch. Deadline. Close.

### Opening (0:00-10:00)

**Synthesis:**
> Five days in. Let's recap what you've built:
>
> - Day 1: [micro-win]
> - Day 2: [micro-win]
> - Day 3: [micro-win]
> - Day 4: [micro-win]
> - Today: [final synthesis]
>
> You have more than you had a week ago. The question today is: what's next?

### Final teach (10:00-30:00)

**Script:**
> The last piece is [FILL — the piece that only the paid offer delivers]. You've done the work to see why it matters. Let me show you what the full path looks like.

### Full pitch (30:00-60:00)

Use the **Pitch** structure from `webinar-script-three-secrets.md`:

- Offer reveal
- Core stack ($[N] each)
- Bonus stack (2nd-best first, best last)
- Total value
- Price reveal
- Payment options
- Guarantee
- Real scarcity (cart close at [time] on Day [N+1])
- CTA

### Q&A (60:00-80:00)

Cover the top 5 objections from ICP Section 10. Every answer bridges back to the offer.

### Close (80:00-90:00)

**Script:**
> The cart closes at [exact time] on [date]. No extensions. That's a promise to the people who said yes today that the deal is the deal.
>
> Five days ago you showed up because [FILL — opening problem]. Today you know the map. The only question is whether you walk it with me or alone. Go to [URL] now.

## Appendix A — Community Management Playbook

### Daily rhythm

| Time | Action | Owner |
|---|---|---|
| 8am | Post daily prompt | Host or CSM |
| 10am | Reply to first wave of posts | Host |
| 2pm | Highlight reel — pin best submissions | CSM |
| 5pm | Evening reminder for tomorrow's session | Automation |
| 9pm | Final engagement round | Host |

### Rules for engagement

- Every attendee post gets a reply within 24h
- Creator shows up personally in first 48h — establishes culture
- CSM or mod handles routine questions after Day 2
- Flag "stuck" attendees for 1:1 outreach

## Appendix C — Performance Targets

| Metric | Target |
|---|---|
| Registrations → Day 1 show-up | 40-55% |
| Day 1 → Day N completion | >= 40% |
| Day N completion → cart click | >= 25% |
| Click → apply | >= 40% |
| Apply → close | 20-35% |
| Challenge → overall close rate on registrants | 3-8% (5-day), 2-5% (3-day), 5-10% (7-day) |

## Appendix D — Pitfalls and Fixes

| Pitfall | Fix |
|---|---|
| Day 1 show-up too low | Better pre-launch content + T-1 personal outreach |
| Mid-challenge drop-off | Stronger daily micro-wins + community engagement |
| Pitch feels abrupt | Soft-seed pitch on Day 3 (5-day) or earlier |
| Q&A drags | Pre-script top 5, time-box, redirect remaining to email |
| Cart-close fatigue | Single deadline, no extension — INV enforced |
