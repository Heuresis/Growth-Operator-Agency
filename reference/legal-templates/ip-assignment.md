Use this when a contractor or collaborator produces a deliverable (copy, design, code, video) that you want to fully own. Pairs with (and is usually referenced from) the Independent Contractor Agreement.

**This IP Assignment** is made on {{EFFECTIVE_DATE}} by {{ASSIGNOR_LEGAL_NAME}} ("**Assignor**") in favor of {{ASSIGNEE_LEGAL_NAME}} ("**Assignee**").

## 1. Background

Assignor has been engaged to produce certain deliverables for Assignee described in {{UNDERLYING_AGREEMENT}} (e.g., the Independent Contractor Agreement dated {{UNDERLYING_DATE}}). The deliverables are listed in **Exhibit 1** (the "**Work**").

## 2. Assignment

Effective on Assignor's receipt of full payment for the Work, Assignor irrevocably and exclusively assigns to Assignee all right, title, and interest in the Work worldwide, including:

- Copyright (and all moral rights Assignor may have, to the extent waivable under applicable law)
- Patent rights (for any patentable subject matter in the Work)
- Trademark rights (where the Work includes proposed marks)
- Trade secret rights
- Rights in databases, data, and compilations
- All other intellectual property rights, now known or hereafter created, in any jurisdiction

Assignee will be the sole and exclusive owner of the Work and may use, reproduce, modify, sublicense, sell, and distribute it freely.

## 3. Work made for hire

To the maximum extent permitted by law, the Work is a "**work made for hire**" as defined in the U.S. Copyright Act, and Assignee is considered the author of the Work. Where Work does not qualify as work made for hire, Section 2 governs.

## 4. Moral rights waiver

Assignor waives, to the maximum extent permitted, any and all moral rights in the Work, including the rights of attribution and integrity. Where waiver is not permitted, Assignor agrees not to assert such rights against Assignee or its licensees.

## 5. Pre-existing IP license

Where the Work incorporates Assignor's pre-existing IP ("**Pre-Existing IP**"), Assignor grants Assignee a perpetual, worldwide, royalty-free, sublicensable, irrevocable license to use, modify, distribute, and create derivative works from that Pre-Existing IP as part of the Work.

A list of Pre-Existing IP incorporated (if any) is attached as **Exhibit 2**. If no Exhibit 2 is attached, Assignor represents that the Work does not incorporate any Pre-Existing IP.

## 6. Representations & warranties

Assignor represents and warrants that:

1. The Work is original to Assignor, except for Pre-Existing IP disclosed in Exhibit 2
2. Assignor has full authority to assign the Work
3. The Work does not infringe any third party's IP rights, privacy rights, or publicity rights
4. The Work does not violate any confidentiality obligation Assignor owes a third party
5. No unlicensed AI-generated, open-source, or third-party content is incorporated without permission and without disclosure to Assignee
6. Assignor has not previously assigned or licensed the Work to a third party in a manner inconsistent with this Assignment

## 7. Further assurances

Assignor agrees to execute documents and take actions Assignee reasonably requests to perfect, register, enforce, or defend the rights assigned, including signing copyright or patent filings. Assignor appoints Assignee as attorney-in-fact to sign on Assignor's behalf if Assignor becomes unavailable or refuses after reasonable notice.

## 8. No-contest

Assignor will not challenge the validity or ownership of the rights assigned, or aid any third party in doing so.

## 9. Indemnity

Assignor indemnifies Assignee against any claim arising from breach of Section 6 (Representations & warranties).
