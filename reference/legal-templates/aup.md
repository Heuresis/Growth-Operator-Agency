## 1. Lawful use

You may not use the Service to:

- Violate any applicable law, regulation, or third-party right
- Infringe intellectual property, trademark, or privacy rights
- Engage in fraud, money-laundering, or tax evasion
- Promote violence, discrimination, or harassment of any protected class
- Sell or promote regulated products outside the permitted jurisdictions (firearms, alcohol, cannabis, gambling, pharmaceuticals, securities — where restricted)

## 2. Security

You may not:

- Attempt to gain unauthorized access to accounts, systems, or data
- Probe, scan, or test the vulnerability of the Service (without our written authorization)
- Circumvent security features, rate limits, or access controls
- Upload malware, viruses, worms, or other malicious code
- Reverse-engineer, decompile, or extract source code
- Use the Service as a jump host to attack other systems

## 3. Content

You may not post, upload, or share:

- Content that is illegal, obscene, defamatory, or sexually explicit
- Hate speech, harassment, or threats directed at any individual or group
- Doxxing or non-consensual personal information
- Content that impersonates another person, brand, or official
- Deceptive or manipulated media intended to mislead
- Spam, unsolicited promotional content, or pyramid-scheme pitches
- Content that violates third-party intellectual property or privacy rights

## 4. Commercial misuse

You may not use the Service to:

- Resell or sublicense access to users outside your seat entitlement
- Scrape, mirror, or systematically extract Service content for a competing product
- Run unsolicited outreach campaigns (cold email to non-opt-ins via our infrastructure)
- Run affiliate promotions in prohibited ways (see Affiliate Agreement if applicable)
- Build a competing product using our frameworks, taxonomies, or code

## 5. Community conduct

In community spaces (Slack, Discord, Circle, Skool, community forums):

- **Assume good intent.** Ask clarifying questions before escalating.
- **Keep it anchored to the work.** The community is a work community, not a general social network.
- **No selling to the room.** Promoting your unrelated offers to the member base is not allowed. Relevant recommendations inside a peer-help thread are fine.
- **Protect privacy.** What's shared in the community stays in the community. Screenshotting named participants to a public audience without consent is a breach.
- **No public callouts of staff.** Use support channels to raise staff concerns.
- **One account per person.** No sockpuppets, no resold logins.
- **No DM spam.** Unsolicited DMs pitching to other members will get you removed.

## 6. Automation & AI

- You may use AI assistants in your own workflow.
- You may not use AI to impersonate a real person, fabricate testimonials, or generate content falsely attributed to a named operator.
- You may not feed confidential Service content into AI tools whose terms grant the provider rights to train on that content (check the AI provider's terms).
- You may not use our Service to train a model that competes with our Service.
