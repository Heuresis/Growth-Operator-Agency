## 2. Subscription & fees

### 2.1 Plans

Available plans and included entitlements are posted at {{PRICING_URL}}. Company may change plan composition or pricing for new customers at any time. Existing subscribers are grandfathered at their current price for at least {{GRANDFATHER_CYCLE_COUNT}} billing cycles from a change announcement.

### 2.2 Billing

- **Cycle:** monthly or annual as selected at signup
- **Charge timing:** on the renewal date of each cycle
- **Failed payment:** 3 retries over {{DUNNING_DAYS}} days; access suspended if not cured
- **Auto-renewal:** plans auto-renew unless cancelled before renewal
- **Cancellation:** cancel anytime from account settings; remaining cycle runs out; no pro-rata refund on remaining cycle unless legally required

If a free trial is offered, it is described on the signup page. Trials convert to paid subscriptions on the end date unless cancelled first. No hidden upgrades.

### 2.4 Upgrades / downgrades

- **Upgrade:** effective immediately, pro-rated charge on current cycle
- **Downgrade:** effective on next cycle start; current cycle retains higher-tier features

## 3. Account

### 3.1 Registration

Customer must provide accurate information and a valid payment method. Customer is responsible for maintaining password security.

### 3.2 Seats & users

Each subscription is for the number of seats described in the plan. Sharing a single account across more users than entitled is a breach. Add seats via the account settings; each additional seat is billed at the plan's per-seat rate.

### 3.3 Admin & users

The primary account owner (Admin) may add, remove, and manage users. Admin actions bind the account.

- Use automated agents or scrapers without written consent
- Transmit malware, illegal content, or content that infringes third-party rights
- Use the Service for spam, phishing, or unsolicited outreach

## 5. Customer data

### 5.1 Ownership

Customer retains ownership of all data Customer uploads or inputs to the Service ("**Customer Data**"). Company holds only a license to process Customer Data to operate and improve the Service.

### 5.2 Data use

Company uses Customer Data to:

- Provide the Service to Customer

- Generate aggregate, anonymized statistics (e.g., "X% of users take action Y") that do not identify any individual customer
- Comply with legal obligations

Company does **not**:

- Sell Customer Data
- Use Customer Data to train general-purpose AI models that would be used across unrelated customers, without opt-in consent
- Share Customer Data with advertisers
