- **Tracking platform:** {{AFFILIATE_PLATFORM_NAME}}. Discrepancies between Affiliate's own tracking and the Company platform are resolved using Company's records.
- **Cross-device:** Attribution relies on cookies. Mobile-to-desktop switches or cookie-blocking may cause attribution loss; Company does not make Affiliate whole for those losses.

## 3. Commission

### 3.1 Rate

| Tier | Condition | Commission |
|---|---|---|
| Standard | Any qualified sale | {{STANDARD_COMMISSION}}% |
| Scaled | Affiliate hits {{TIER2_THRESHOLD}} sales / calendar month | {{TIER2_COMMISSION}}% for all sales that month |
| Lifetime | For first {{LIFETIME_MONTHS}} months on continuity products | {{LIFETIME_COMMISSION}}% |

### 3.2 What qualifies

A sale qualifies when:
- The buyer clicks Affiliate's tracking link before purchase
- The cookie is active at purchase (within {{COOKIE_WINDOW_DAYS}} days)
- The sale completes (card charged, order confirmed)
- The refund window has passed (see 3.4)

### 3.3 What does not qualify

- Affiliate's own purchases (self-referral)
- Purchases by Affiliate's employees, spouse, or household members
- Purchases attributed to a prior affiliate with a later click-in-window
- Free, discounted, or complimentary enrollments
- Subscription upgrades not connected to Affiliate's link
- Sales generated through prohibited methods (Section 5)

### 3.4 Clawback

If a sale is refunded, disputed, or charged back, the commission is clawed back from the next payout or, if the balance is insufficient, Affiliate owes the amount to Company within 30 days.

### 3.5 Payout

- **Schedule:** Net-{{PAYOUT_NET_DAYS}}. Commissions earned in month M are paid by the {{PAYOUT_DAY_OF_MONTH}} of month M + 1, after the refund window clears.
- **Minimum payout:** {{MINIMUM_PAYOUT_AMOUNT}}. Below minimum, balance rolls forward.
- **Method:** {{PAYOUT_METHOD}} (PayPal / bank transfer / Wise / equivalent).
- **Tax:** Affiliate is responsible for tax on commissions received. Where Company is required to withhold (e.g., US W-8BEN / W-9), Affiliate must submit the required form before payout.

## 4. Approved marketing

Affiliate may:

- Promote via owned email list (Affiliate's own opt-in list only)
- Promote via owned social / YouTube / podcast / blog
- Run paid ads on platforms Company allows (see 4.1)
- Use Company-supplied creative (swipe copy, banners, videos) without alteration
- Write original honest reviews

### 4.1 Paid-ad rules

- **Brand bidding is prohibited.** Affiliate may not bid on Company's brand name, misspellings, or Company's executives' names on Google, Bing, Meta, or any search network.
- **Typosquatting and look-alike domains are prohibited.**
- **Affiliate's landing page must be clearly Affiliate's**, not a clone of Company's sales page.
- **Disclosures must be FTC-compliant.** "#ad" / "Affiliate" / "I earn a commission" in clear, unavoidable position at the start of the post.

## 5. Prohibited practices

Any of the following voids attribution and may terminate the Agreement:

- Spamming (cold email without consent, unsolicited DMs, forum-scraping)
- Incentive violations (paying people to click, cash-back, cashback-via-own-site)
- Cookie stuffing, forced clicks, invisible iframes
- Trademark abuse, passing off as Company, claiming Company employment
- False claims (guarantee of results, medical claims, income claims not permitted by Company)
- Fake testimonials or reviews
- Promoting Company in contexts that damage Company's reputation (adult, hate, illegal goods)
- Undisclosed AI-generated impersonation of real operators
- Using Company's copyrighted materials outside the approved swipe file

## 6. FTC, GDPR, and jurisdictional compliance

Affiliate represents that Affiliate will:

- Include clear affiliate disclosure on every piece of promotional content (FTC Endorsement Guides, UK ASA, EU UCPD, AUS ACCC as applicable)
- Obtain consent for marketing emails under GDPR / CASL / equivalents
- Not make income claims that imply typical earnings
- Not run promotions in jurisdictions where Company's product is unavailable
- Comply with platform-specific disclosure rules (YouTube, TikTok, Instagram)

Failure to disclose appropriately is a material breach.

## 7. Intellectual property

Affiliate receives a non-exclusive, non-transferable, revocable license to use Company's brand, logo, product screenshots, and swipe copy strictly for promotion of the approved products during the Term. Any other use requires written consent.

Affiliate may not:
- Alter Company's logo or brand marks
- Claim Company's frameworks as Affiliate's own
- File a trademark on a Company-related name or mark
- Register a domain containing Company's brand without written consent

## 8. Term & termination

- **Term:** Begins on {{EFFECTIVE_DATE}} and continues until terminated.
- **Termination for convenience:** Either Party may terminate with {{TERMINATION_NOTICE_DAYS}} days' written notice.
