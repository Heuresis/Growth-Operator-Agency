**This Mutual Non-Disclosure Agreement** ("**Agreement**") is entered on {{EFFECTIVE_DATE}} between {{PARTY_A_LEGAL_NAME}} ("**Party A**") and {{PARTY_B_LEGAL_NAME}} ("**Party B**"), collectively the "**Parties**".

The Parties expect to share non-public information to explore a potential business relationship or work together on an engagement ("**Purpose**"). This Agreement governs how that information is handled.

## 1. Confidential Information

"**Confidential Information**" means any non-public information disclosed by one Party (the "**Disclosing Party**") to the other (the "**Receiving Party**"), in any form, that is designated confidential, marked confidential, or that a reasonable person would treat as confidential given its nature and the context of disclosure.

Examples include, without limitation:
- Customer lists, prospect lists, revenue numbers, unit economics, pricing
- Offer construction, funnel architecture, scripts, ad creative
- Strategic plans, launch plans, product roadmaps
- Source code, credentials, API keys
- Personnel matters, partnership discussions
- Anything a Party explicitly marks "Confidential"

## 2. Exclusions

Confidential Information does not include information that:

1. Is or becomes publicly available without fault of the Receiving Party
2. The Receiving Party lawfully possessed before disclosure, without a duty of confidentiality
3. The Receiving Party rightfully receives from a third party without a duty of confidentiality
4. The Receiving Party independently develops without reference to the Confidential Information
5. Is required to be disclosed by law, court order, or regulatory authority — in which case the Receiving Party will give the Disclosing Party prompt notice where permitted and cooperate in seeking a protective order

## 3. Use & non-disclosure

The Receiving Party will:

1. Use Confidential Information only for the Purpose
2. Protect Confidential Information with the same degree of care it uses for its own confidential information, and no less than reasonable care
3. Limit access to employees, contractors, and advisors who have a need to know and are bound by confidentiality obligations at least as strict as this Agreement
4. Not reverse-engineer, decompile, or attempt to derive trade secrets from Confidential Information
5. Not use Confidential Information to compete with the Disclosing Party or circumvent the relationship

## 4. Term

This Agreement takes effect on the Effective Date and continues for {{TERM_YEARS}} years. Obligations with respect to Confidential Information survive termination for an additional {{SURVIVAL_YEARS}} years (perpetually for trade secrets to the extent recognized under applicable law).

## 5. Return or destruction

On written request, or on termination of the Purpose, the Receiving Party will within {{RETURN_DAYS}} days:

- Return all tangible Confidential Information
- Delete or destroy all electronic copies (including backups and AI-assistant conversation histories the Receiving Party controls)
- Certify in writing that it has done so

A Party may retain one archival copy for legal or regulatory compliance, which remains subject to this Agreement indefinitely.

## 6. No license, no obligation

Nothing in this Agreement grants either Party a license to the other Party's intellectual property. This Agreement does not obligate either Party to enter into a further transaction, provide any services, or purchase anything.

## 7. Remedies

The Parties acknowledge that breach of this Agreement may cause irreparable harm for which damages are inadequate. The Disclosing Party may seek injunctive relief without posting bond, in addition to other legal remedies.
