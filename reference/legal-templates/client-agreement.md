## 3. Client responsibilities

Client will:

- **Submit deliverables on time.** Late submissions may not receive review in the session.
- **Provide accurate context.** Honest reporting of metrics, constraints, and decisions. The Program's value depends on diagnostic accuracy.
- **Execute the moves agreed in session.** The Program is advisory; execution is Client's responsibility. No execution = no outcome.
- **Respect staff, coaches, and fellow participants.** Harassment, discrimination, or hostile conduct is grounds for immediate termination with no refund.
