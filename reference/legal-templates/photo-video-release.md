## 2. Edits, combinations, & context

Company may edit, combine, overlay, caption, subtitle, translate, and re-purpose the Content. Company will not edit in a way that materially misrepresents what Releasor said. Company may place the Content adjacent to third-party content in Company's marketing.

## 3. Revocation

Releasor may request revocation of consent for **future** use by emailing {{LEGAL_CONTACT_EMAIL}} with the subject line *"Release Revocation"*. Within 30 days, Company will stop using the Content in newly created materials.

Materials already created, printed, scheduled, or distributed (including running paid ads that cannot be recalled within reasonable operational windows) may continue to run through their normal lifecycle. Archived content on platforms where removal is impractical may remain.

## 4. Statements of fact

To the extent Releasor's Content includes statements of fact (revenue numbers, results, time-to-outcome), Releasor represents that those statements are accurate at the time given. If they later become inaccurate, Company may continue to use them with a clarifying caption or timestamp.
