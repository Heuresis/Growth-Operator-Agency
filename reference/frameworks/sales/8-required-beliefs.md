# 8 Required Beliefs — the VSL director

> **Source:** the VSL director (a sales-training operation / Funnel Mastery) — universal belief-installation framework
> **Status:** First-class framework. Referenced by /build-vsl, /webinar-script, /design-offer, /email-sequence.

## The Thesis

**No one buys anything until they believe 8 things.** If any belief is missing, the sale doesn't close. Installing all 8 through the customer journey is what converts — not "urgency" or "scarcity" in isolation.

## The 8 Beliefs

| # | Belief | What they must believe |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Reproduction** | "I want this outcome" (derivative-desire check — do they actually want it?) |
| 2 | **Derivative desire** | "This outcome connects to my deeper desire" (why the surface-outcome matters) |
| 3 | **Can-fulfill** | "This offer CAN deliver the outcome" (capability) |
| 4 | **Urgency** | "I need this NOW, not later" (why-now) |
| 5 | **This-offer** | "THIS specific offer is the right one" (vs generic category) |
| 6 | **Better/faster** | "This beats my alternatives" (vs competitor / DIY / other methods) |
| 7 | **Trust-person** | "I trust the creator personally" (character) |
| 8 | **Trust-expertise** | "I trust their expertise actually delivers" (competence) |

### Webinar (7-Figure Script)
- **Intro 15-30 min:** beliefs 1 + 2 + 7
- **Content 45-60 min:** beliefs 3 + 5 + 6 + 8
- **Transition:** belief 4
- **Pitch:** beliefs 3 + 5 + 6 (reinforced) + guarantee (7+3)
- **Q&A:** belief 4 + 5 finalized

### Offer Document (/design-offer)
- Every component maps to one or more beliefs
- Value Stack → beliefs 3 + 6
- Bonuses → belief 4 (urgency) + 5 (this offer)
- Guarantee → beliefs 3 + 7 + 8
- Pricing → belief 5 (this offer)
- Unique Mechanism → beliefs 3 + 5 + 6

### Email Nurture Sequence
Week 1 emails 1-2: beliefs 1 + 2
Week 2 emails 3-4: beliefs 3 + 4
Week 3 emails 5-6: beliefs 5 + 6
Week 4 emails 7-8: beliefs 7 + 8

Interlaced with value teaching + Core Belief Statement reinforcement.

## Diagnostic Application (when conversion drops)

If conversion underperforms, diagnose WHICH belief failed:
- Low opt-ins → belief 1 (hook fails to trigger want)
- Low watch time → belief 2 or 7 (connection gap)
- High watch but low CTA click → belief 3 or 5 (doubt in capability or this-offer)
- Low checkout completion → belief 4 or 6 (no urgency or not-better-enough)
- High refund rate → belief 8 (expertise under-delivered post-purchase)

## Invariants

- All 8 beliefs MUST be installed before the close
- No belief = no sale (multiplicative — missing one drops conversion dramatically)
- Install in sequence (1-2-3-4-5-6-7-8) — out-of-order = friction
- Belief-install density varies by runtime (VSL = compressed, webinar = distributed, email = slow)
- Verify via **Belief Installation Map** before ship
