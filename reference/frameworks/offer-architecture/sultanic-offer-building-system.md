## The 7 Steps of Phase 2 (Offer Architecture)

Phase 2 sits inside a larger 5-phase process (see companion doc `phase-0-5-full.md` — pending). Phase 2 is the offer-architecture core:

### Step 1 — Outcome Definition
Specify the transformation in **identity-level** language, not functional.

- Functional: "lose 20 lbs"
- Identity: "become the version of yourself who doesn't think about food"

Identity-level outcomes command higher prices + deeper buyer commitment.

### Step 2 — Mechanism Identification
Name the unique mechanism (not method). Per `primitives/unique-mechanism.md`:
- 2-5 word proprietary term
- Noun-form (System / Method / Protocol / Engine / Architecture)
- Two-part (problem mechanism + solution mechanism)
- Specific differentiator (what nobody else claims)

### Step 3 — Capability / Resource Removal
List what the offer **REMOVES** from the buyer's required effort. Pulled from ICP Compartment 8 Capability Audit:

- What skills do they lack? → offer provides them OR removes need
- What resources do they lack? → offer bundles them OR substitutes
- What's blocking? → offer clears

This becomes CORE offer components (not bonuses).

### Step 4 — Feedback Loop Engineering
Design the **first-win milestone** — when and how the buyer experiences initial success.

Rules:
- First-win within 7-14 days of purchase
- Specific (measurable, not "feel progress")
- Demonstrable to themselves AND others (social component)
- Engineered (don't leave to chance)

First-win is the #1 retention + advocacy driver. Also feeds `/retention-check` Thriving criteria.

### Step 5 — Risk Reversal Architecture
Structure the guarantee to be **ROI-positive**. Tiers (from `sales/value-stack-architecture.md`):

- Tier 1 — Unconditional refund (weak, baseline)
- Tier 2 — Conditional outcome guarantee
- Tier 3 — Reversed-risk (work-with-until-outcome)
- Tier 4 — Better-than-zero (refund + keep valuable bonus)
- Tier 5 — Pay-for-results

Match guarantee tier to offer tier:
- $997 offer → Tier 1-2
- $3K-$10K → Tier 2-3
- $10K+ → Tier 3-5

### Step 6 — Pricing Structure
Pricing is **identity-aligned**, not cost-plus.

- Cost-plus thinking: "how much did it cost me to deliver + margin"
- Identity-aligned: "who buys at this price, and what do they require to feel congruent?"

Examples:
- $5K offer buyer = middle-market business owner. Offer needs professional feel, reasonable community.
- $50K offer buyer = established founder. Offer needs exclusivity, bespoke treatment, elite community.

**Rule:** cheap prices don't transform identity. High price = part of the buy.

### Step 7 — Upsell Ecosystem
5-10 adjacent solutions that amplify the core transformation.

Ecosystem design:
- Lead magnet (free entry)
- Tripwire ($7-$97 filter)
- **Core offer** (the primary purchase)
- Upsell 1 (immediate post-core — related problem the buyer now has)
- Upsell 2 (30-90 days post — ascension to deeper involvement)
- Ultra ($30K+ — annual / retreat / 1:1)
- Continuity ($297/mo — ongoing monthly value)

Each tier:
- Delivers complete value at that price
- Sets up the next tier naturally
- Has clear identity markers

## Cross-Framework Integration

Sultanic's 7-step pairs with Hormozi's Value Equation:
- Steps 1-2 (Outcome + Mechanism) amplify **Dream Outcome × Perceived Likelihood** (numerator)
- Steps 3-5 (Capability Removal + First-Win + Guarantee) reduce **Time Delay × Effort** (denominator)

Target: Value Equation score ≥ 150 post-7-step.

## The Economics Validation Layer

Per INV-4, every offer must show 3:1 LTV:CAC before ship:

- LTV = AOV × retention × upsells × referrals
- CAC = realistic for creator's channel mix
- Payback period < 6 months (high-ticket target)
- Gross margin ≥ 70%

If economics fail, iterate Steps 6-7 (pricing + upsell ecosystem) — OR acknowledge channel-mix change needed.

## Sultanic Invariants (applied to offers)

- Pricing is identity-aligned, not cost-plus
- Traffic is King, Offer is Queen — Queen must exist before King
- "They buy the IDENTITY that uses this, not the thing itself"
- Same offer repackaged for different ICP = different product (Air-Bending Element 4)
- Risk reversal creates the sale; product delivers it
