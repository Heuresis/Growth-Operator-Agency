# Trister 13-Step VSL — Variant C

> **Source:** Steven Trister — 13-Step VSL Framework (slide-based, voice-over delivery)
> **Best for:** Data-heavy niches (SaaS, finance, analytical B2B), high-production feel, no-camera creators, evergreen repeatability

## The 13 Steps

| # | Step | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Your Bold Claim** | Specific outcome promise |
| 2 | **Snippets of ROI** | Fast hits of proof (numbers + results) |
| 3 | **Pain Points & Struggles** | Amplify the status quo cost |
| 4 | **Future Pace Your Bold Claim** | Show the after-state |
| 5 | **Social Proof** | Testimonials + case studies |
| 6 | **Company Credibility** | Credentials, press, years, clients |
| 7 | **Company Introduction** | Who you are, origin |
| 8 | **Main Benefits** | Top 3-5 benefits |
| 9 | **High-Level Features** | Named mechanism + 3-5 sub-steps |
| 10 | **Staying As You Are** | Cost of inaction (agitation) |
| 11 | **Remind Them Of Your Bold Claim** | Closing the loop from Step 1 |
| 12 | **More Social Proof** | Final proof stack |
| 13 | **Call To Action** | Single specific CTA |

## Key Differentiators from Pampa/Hogendoorn

- **Slide-based delivery** — not face-on-camera, not narrative
- **Voice-over first** — scripted to be read aloud, not performed
- **Data/logic dominant** — fits analytical buyer psychology
- **Claim-repeat structure** — bold claim at Step 1, reminded at Step 11, CTA Step 13
- **Credibility layer explicit** (Step 6) — company credentials as proof pillar

## Production Stack

- **Slide design** — clean, high-contrast, one idea per slide
- **Voice-over delivery** — measured pace, clear diction
- **Recording setup** — clean audio > video quality
- **Editing** — slide timing synced to voice, B-roll only if supports point

See Trister source: `21a39dd823e78187a51fd8c42270d3ca` (Recording), `21a39dd823e781cfbca8d9f61390789a` (Editing).

## Trister's Warning

> *"Do not copy verbatim — VSL won't be unique. Get creative."*

The 13-step is scaffolding, not script. Fill with creator-specific content + voice + mechanism.
