# the VSL director 15-Step VSL — Variant A

> **Source:** the VSL director — *8-Figure VSL Blueprint* · Proof: $129M+ (The Real World), $50M+ (Jordan Lee), $25M+ (Digital Launchpad), $10M+ (Champs Only)
> **Target conversion:** 10%+
> **Runtime:** 20-45 min typical

## The 15 Steps

| # | Step | Purpose | Belief installed |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Hook** | Pattern interrupt + title-promise delivery | #1 Reproduction |
| 2 | **Lead** | Deepen the desire, connect to aspirational identity | #2 Derivative desire |
| 3 | **Qualification** | "Is this for me?" — self-select in/out | #1 reinforced |
| 4 | **Problem / Question** | Amplify pain; create urgency | #4 Urgency (start) |
| 5 | **Hero Story + Guru Intro** | Creator's origin story + authority | #7 Trust person |
| 6 | **Solution Introduction** | Name the mechanism + WHAT it does | #3 Can fulfill + #5 This offer |
| 7 | **Features / Benefits** | Components + transformation mapping | #3 + #6 Better/faster |
| 8 | **Testimonials / Receipts** | Isomorphic case studies + proof | #3 + #7 + #8 Trust expertise |
| 9 | **Price Anchoring** | Stated value vs investment (see `sales/value-stack-architecture.md`) | #5 + #6 |
| 10 | **Guarantee + Risk Reversal** | ROI-positive guarantee | #3 + #7 (trust deepened) |
| 11 | **Urgency Story** | Why-now, specific trigger | #4 finalized |
| 12 | **Crossroads Close** | Force decision (action vs inaction — see `sales/crossroads-close.md`) | Decision moment |
| 13 | **Takeaway Close** | "This isn't for everyone" + re-qualify | #5 + #6 reinforced |
| 14 | **Help Them Buy** | Reduce friction — payment options + how-to-checkout | Removes barrier |
| 15 | **Future Pace** | Identity after purchase — the life they step into | #1 + #2 reinforced |

## The 5-Phase Psychological Compression

For shorter VSLs (8-12 min paid-cold variant), compress the 15 steps into 5 phases:

| Phase | Time | Mapped steps |
|---|---|---|
| Pattern Interrupt | 0-3 min | Steps 1-3 |
| Identity Crisis | 3-8 min | Steps 4-5 |
| New Worldview | 8-15 min | Steps 6-8 |
| Future Vision | 15-22 min | Steps 9-10 + Testimonials |
| Decision Stack | 22-30 min | Steps 11-15 |

## Critical Invariants

- **Never sell before Step 9.** Copy-invariant — revealing price/offer before step 9 kills conversion.
- **Mechanism threads 5 places:** Hook / Problem / Solution / Testimonials / Close.
- **Testimonials must be isomorphic** (same starting situation, same outcome, same process).
- **Crossroads Close is non-optional** (Step 12).
- **Future Pace always last** (Step 15) — identity-level close, not benefit recap.

## Production Rules (15-step VSL methodology)

- On-camera, on-location filmed (never remote for world-class VSLs)
- Record every take
- 99% text-based review comms (Telegram), minimal meetings
- Special-forces team, not corporate bloat
- Post-production: cut ruthlessly — every sentence earns the next
