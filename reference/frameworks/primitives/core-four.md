# Core Four — The Primitive

> **Source:** the acquisition economist, *$100M Leads* (Chapter 5 — "The Core Four")
> **Status:** First-class primitive. The 2×2 that governs every lead-generation decision.

## The Equation

Every way to get a lead falls into ONE of four quadrants:

```
           │  1:1 (personal)         │  1:many (broadcast)
───────────┼─────────────────────────┼───────────────────────────
  Warm     │  ① Warm Outreach        │  ② Post Content
  (know)   │     (DM existing        │     (to your audience /
           │      network, friends,  │      existing list)
           │      past customers)    │
───────────┼─────────────────────────┼───────────────────────────
  Cold     │  ③ Cold Outreach        │  ④ Run Paid Ads
  (don't   │     (DM strangers, cold │     (+ organic content
   know)   │      email, door-to-    │      to cold audiences)
           │      door)              │
```

**Every lead on earth comes from one of these four.** All other labels ("SEO," "LinkedIn," "YouTube," "Funnels") collapse into one or combinations of these four.

## The Rule

**Pick 1-2 at a time. Master before adding.**

Running all four poorly > running two well. Split focus = fractional effectiveness across the board.

## Matching the Core Four to Creator Stage

| Stage | Recommended Core Four |
|---|---|
| Pre-revenue, no network | Start with ① Warm Outreach (friends + past contacts) |
| $10-30K MRR, small list | ① Warm Outreach + ② Post Content (to small existing audience) |
| $30-100K MRR, proven offer | ② Post Content + ④ Paid Ads (scale what works) |
| $100K+ MRR | All 4 + Lead Getters (exponential layer — see below) |

## The Lead Getters (Exponential Layer — the acquisition economist)

Once one of the Core Four is producing, **Lead Getters** multiply the output:
1. **Customers** — get referrals from buyers
2. **Employees** — hire setters/closers who outreach at scale
3. **Agencies** — pay external teams to run outreach/ads
4. **Affiliates** — pay others to bring leads

Core Four is linear (you do it). Lead Getters is exponential (others do it for you). Don't skip to Lead Getters before Core Four is dialed — you'll burn capital.

## Per-Quadrant Tactics

### ① Warm Outreach (1:1 Warm)
- DM past customers asking for referrals
- Personal check-ins with network
- One-on-one calls with warm leads
- LinkedIn DMs to 2nd-degree connections
- Email past buyers with new offer
- Highest conversion rate. Lowest scalability.

### ② Post Content (1:many Warm)
- LinkedIn / X / IG / YouTube posts to existing followers
- Email broadcasts to list
- Community posts (Skool / Circle / Discord)
- Newsletter
- Podcast to your audience
- Medium conversion + medium scale.

### ③ Cold Outreach (1:1 Cold)
- Cold DMs to strangers
- Cold emails
- Cold calls
- Door-to-door / in-person outreach
- Low conversion + low scale + high volume-driven.

### ④ Paid Ads / Organic to Cold (1:many Cold)
- Meta Ads / Google / YouTube / TikTok / LinkedIn
- Organic content to cold audiences (SEO, algorithmic reach)
- PR / press
- Affiliates driving to creator's ads
- Medium-high conversion (with specificity) + unlimited scale + capital-required.

## Decision Framework

When someone asks "how do I get more leads?":

1. **Which of the Core Four is currently producing?** (map existing activity)
2. **Are you doing it well enough?** (benchmark against peers)
3. **If no:** tighten your current quadrant before adding another
4. **If yes:** which quadrant should come next based on stage?
5. **Have you added Lead Getters?** (only if Core Four is already producing)

## Anti-Patterns

- ❌ Running all 4 simultaneously while starting out (fractional effort)
- ❌ Jumping to paid ads before organic works (expensive learning)
- ❌ Scaling a broken funnel via Lead Getters (multiplies the leak)
- ❌ Measuring vanity metrics per quadrant (followers, impressions) vs leads generated

## Validation

Core Four strategy is dialed when:
- [ ] Current revenue attributable to specific Core Four quadrant(s)
- [ ] CPL + conversion benchmarks tracked per quadrant
- [ ] LTV:CAC >= 3 per quadrant (not just blended)
- [ ] Clear next quadrant + rationale for expansion
