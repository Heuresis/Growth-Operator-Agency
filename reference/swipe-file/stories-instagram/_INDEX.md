## The 4-Layer Anatomy — Reminder

Every IG story sequence should run through 4 layers each day:

1. **Hook** — pattern interrupt in the first frame
2. **Value** — educational / story / entertainment payload
3. **Connection** — direct ask (reply, poll, question box)
4. **CTA** — single action (link, DM trigger, swipe up)

See `anatomy-4-layer.md` for the full breakdown.

---

## Frame Economics

Instagram Stories:
- Each frame = 15 seconds video or 5 seconds static
- 5-10 frames per day sweet spot
- First 3 frames carry 80% of the attention — invest accordingly
- Drop-off compounds: frame 10 = ~20% of frame 1 audience

## When To Pick Which Sequence

### Pick Launch when
- Cart is opening in 7-14 days
- Offer is anchored to a specific window
- Audience is already warm

### Pick Lead Magnet when
- New asset (PDF, checklist, mini-course) is ready
- Goal: email list growth or DM funnel
- Soft-top-of-funnel

### Pick Daily Value when
- Nothing specific to sell this week
- Building authority compound
- Maintaining feed presence

### Pick Behind-Scenes when
- Trust-building with a warm audience
- New product/service in design phase
- Humanization needed

### Pick Case Study when
- Fresh client result ready (with permission)
- Proof-forward week
- Bridge to offer

### Pick Community Building when
- Engagement rate plateau
- New followers need activation
- Wanting to collect audience signal

### Pick Offer Tease when
- Warming toward a launch in 3-6 weeks
- Consultative offer (not productized)
- Audience needs more signal before a hard CTA

---

## Sequence Cadence

Don't run two sequences back-to-back. Mix types:

- Week 1: Daily Value
- Week 2: Case Study
- Week 3: Launch (if applicable)
- Week 4: Community Building
- Repeat

---

## Voice-Match Priority

IG stories are the highest-frequency touchpoint with a warm audience. Voice-match is critical:
- 3+ `phrases_to_use` across the week
- Visual consistency (palette, font, frame style)
- Tone matches the creator's native voice (direct / consultative / storytelling / contrarian)
