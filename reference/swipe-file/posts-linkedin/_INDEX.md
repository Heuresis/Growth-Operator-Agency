## When To Pick Which Pattern

### Pick PIER when
- You have a clean problem + proof + recommendation
- The post is in the Authority pillar (50% of weekly mix)
- You want to teach + drive saves

### Pick Hook-Loop-CTA when
- Default choice; any pillar
- You're using a strong hook from `hooks/` swipe
- You want explicit scroll-to-CTA journey

### Pick Thought-Proof-CTA when
- You have a sharp opinion backed by dated receipts
- The post is offer-tied or case-study-adjacent
- You want to signal "I did this, here's proof"

### Pick Contrarian-Reframe when
- You have a reversal in positioning doc's Big Enemy
- The post is in Engagement pillar (30%)
- You want comment-driven reach

### Pick Personal-to-Business when
- You have a specific personal story that rhymes with a business lesson
- The post humanizes the creator
- The audience is deeper in the funnel (LinkedIn readers who already know the creator)

### Pick Listicle when
- You have 3-10 discrete items that cohere under one theme
- You want maximum save rate
- The post is teaching / authority pillar

### Pick Day-in-Life when
- You want transparency / trust signal
- The creator has an unusual or envied workflow
- The audience is considering buying in

### Pick Failure-Lesson when
- You have a specific, recent failure
- The lesson wraps around a mechanism the offer solves
- You want humility-based authority

## The 30/50/20 Weekly Distribution

Per `write-linkedin-post` skill, LinkedIn posts split:

| Pillar | % | Post Types |
|---|---|---|
| **Engagement** | 30% | Contrarian-Reframe, Day-in-Life (light) |
| **Authority** | 50% | PIER, Listicle, Thought-Proof-CTA, Personal-to-Business |
| **Conversion** | 20% | Hook-Loop-CTA (offer-tied), Failure-Lesson (with mechanism) |

A sample week:
- Mon: PIER (Authority)
- Tue: Contrarian-Reframe (Engagement)
- Thu: Listicle (Authority)
- Fri: Hook-Loop-CTA offer-tied (Conversion)

Three-to-four posts per week. No daily.
