## When To Pick Which

### Pick Educational when
- You're teaching a concept, not telling a story
- Audience is broad, insight is compact
- Default choice for authority-building threads

### Pick Listicle when
- 5-10 discrete items cohere under one theme
- Save-optimized target
- "N [things] that [outcome]" hook pattern

### Pick Case Study when
- You have a specific dated client or self result
- You want to teach a mechanism via concrete example
- Proof-forward posts

### Pick Contrarian when
- You have a sharp reversal (3-5 tweets max)
- Polarization = engagement
- Big Enemy positioning

### Pick Step-by-Step when
- You have a linear process with 5-10 steps
- Reader should be able to execute after reading
- How-to / tactical thread

### Pick Personal Narrative when
- You have a real story with tension + turn + aftermath
- Humanization + authority combo
- Longer threads (10-15 tweets)

### Pick Curation when
- You're recommending resources with editorial commentary
- Community value / generosity angle
- Best for established accounts

## Thread Cadence

2-3 threads per week per the skill. Single tweets batched separately via Daily Tweets (covered under skill, not swipe).

Golden hour: 9-11am + 6-8pm ET (adjust for audience timezone).

Reply-to-thread T+24-48h. Quote-tweet T+48-72h. Both lift reach ~2×.

---

## The First Tweet Law

First 50 chars visible on many clients. The hook must open in that window. 

Specificity ≥ 8. Number or named-entity or bold claim. Never "A thread 🧵" as the full hook.

---

## The Last Tweet Law

Single CTA only. One action.

Follow / DM / Link-in-reply / Book a call (offer-tied threads only — 20% rule).

No stacking.
