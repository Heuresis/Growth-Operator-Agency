# Verticals — Industry Customization Packs

> **Purpose:** each vertical pack is a markdown brief that a creator in that industry reads to customize the Growth Operating Agency workspace to their niche. The workspace skills and schema are vertical-agnostic. These packs are the vertical overlay.

## How to use

1. Identify the vertical closest to the creator's actual business (use the decision tree below).
2. Read the vertical pack end to end.
3. Use the ICP archetype, offer archetype, pricing benchmarks, and objection patterns from the pack to seed `company.yaml` compartments 1-3.
4. Use the typical funnel and content angles to inform `/build-funnel` and `/content-calendar`.
5. Use the common objections to prepare `/objection-library` and `/sales-script` work.
6. When a creator sits between two verticals, read both and take the applicable parts from each.

## Decision tree — which vertical applies?

Start at the top. Answer each question honestly. Land on the closest pack.

1. **Does the creator serve businesses or individuals?**
   - Businesses → go to 2
   - Individuals → go to 6

2. **For businesses, is the creator selling advisory/coaching or services?**
   - Coaching/advisory → go to 3
   - Services (done-for-you / productized) → go to 5

3. **Who is the business customer — founder/CEO or a specific functional role?**
   - Founder/CEO → `business-coaching.md`
   - Sales role → `sales-training.md`
   - Other functional role → adapt from `consulting-agency.md`

4. **Is the coaching focused on a specific revenue-generating discipline?**
   - If real-estate investing → `real-estate-investing.md`
   - Otherwise → `business-coaching.md`

5. **What does the service deliver to the brand?**
   - Ecom / DTC services → `ecom-agency.md`
   - Other productized consulting → `consulting-agency.md`

6. **For individuals, what does the creator's promise primarily transform?**
   - Body / physical health and fitness → `fitness-coaching.md`
   - Body / medical wellness (hormones / gut / sleep / etc.) → `health-wellness.md`
   - Mind / identity / beliefs → `mindset-coaching.md`
   - Career / work transition → `career-pivot.md`
   - Knowledge / skill (broad info) → `info-course-creator.md`

## When the creator doesn't fit cleanly

- Creator sits between two verticals → read both packs, take the overlapping parts, flag the mismatch in `company.yaml` notes.
- Creator creates a new vertical (first-mover) → use the pack closest in audience + price tier as scaffolding, override specifics.
- Creator serves multiple verticals → pick the primary revenue vertical; note the others for cross-sell ideas.

## What's in each vertical pack

Every pack covers:

1. **Audience primer** — who the customer is in this vertical, what they believe, what they already spend on, how they search
2. **Sample ICP** — a partially-filled ICP Document scaffold tuned to this vertical
3. **Offer archetype** — the typical offer shape, value stack components, bonuses that work, guarantee patterns
4. **Pricing benchmarks** — range of prices for entry / mid / high tiers, with context
5. **Typical funnel** — the default funnel archetype for this vertical and why
6. **Content angles** — hook patterns, content pillars, and platforms that work in this vertical
7. **Proof patterns** — how trust is built in this vertical
8. **Common objections** — top 10 objections specific to this vertical
9. **Pricing ladder** — lead magnet through ultra-tier for this vertical
10. **Common pitfalls + fixes** — vertical-specific failure modes

## What's NOT in each pack

- Real competitor names — packs use archetype descriptors
- Real creator / client names — packs use archetype descriptors
- Copyrighted framework names — packs use descriptors
- Regulatory advice specific to jurisdiction — creators must verify with a licensed professional (especially health, finance, real estate, legal)
