# ClipScale Academy - 3-Tier Offer Architecture

> **Case:** ClipScale Academy, a Phase-3 instantiation of the Growth OS foundations pipeline
> **Built from:** Signal Report (85/100 viability) → ICP (90/100 completeness) → Positioning → Offer
> **Framework applied:** the offer architect's Phase 2 Pricing Architecture (tiered default) + Value Equation scoring
> **Outcome:** Offer validated with LTV:CAC 6.9:1, Value Equation 5.25, gross margin 63%

## Context

ClipScale Academy teaches aspiring clippers to build $10K/month clipping agencies serving Twitch, YouTube, and Kick streamers. The market is Solution Aware but has no category leader - ClipFlow ($49/month on Whop, 16.3K members) is the incumbent at the low end; nothing exists at the premium band. The ICP is HELPLESS-dominant (60%, "I don't know how to find clients") with WORTHLESS secondary (40%, "I should be further ahead by now"). This offer was engineered to serve that ICP through the offer architect's tiered pricing architecture - three options (DIY / DWY / DFY) at a 1X / 3X / 7.5X pricing ratio, with Tier 2 positioned as "most popular" and the ascension path visible.

## The Encoded Offer

**Transformation Statement:** "Go from posting random clips for pennies on Whop to running a $10K/month clipping agency with 4-5 retainer clients and a team of editors in 90 days."

**Unique Mechanism:** The Clip-to-Agency Method™ - a 3-phase system: (1) Production Mastery (AI+human hybrid workflow producing clips 3× faster than manual), (2) Client Engine (DM scripts, proposal templates, pricing frameworks specifically for approaching Twitch/YouTube/Kick streamers), (3) Scale System (team building, SOPs, quality control, client retention).

**Value Equation Score (acquisition-economics methodology):** (Dream Outcome 9 × Perceived Likelihood 7) / (Time Delay 3 × Effort 4) = 63/12 = **5.25** - above the 5.0 threshold, strong perceived value.

---

### Tier 1: Clip Starter (DIY) - $997

DIY self-paced course + community access. Lifetime course access, 6 months community.

**Deliverables:**
1. The Clip-to-Agency Method™ video curriculum - 8 modules covering Phases 1-2 of the method
2. The Streamer Client Outreach Kit - 10 proven DM scripts, 3 proposal templates, pricing calculator, objection-handling guides
3. The AI+Human Hybrid Workflow Blueprint - setup guide for Opus Clip + CapCut/Premiere + posting calendar
4. The Clip Agency Starter Pack - contract templates, invoice templates, onboarding checklist
5. Private community access

**Anchor:** $4,200 (comparable to SMMA courses + individual tool guides + contract templates purchased separately)
**Timeline to Results:** First client outreach within 7 days. First paying client within 30-45 days.

---

### Tier 2: Clip Accelerator (DWY) - $2,997 [TARGET TIER]

Course + weekly group coaching + hot-seat calls + community. Lifetime course access, 12 weeks of live coaching, 12 months community.

**Deliverables (everything in Tier 1, plus):**
1. Weekly group coaching calls (12 sessions) - live hot-seats diagnosing and solving specific situations in real-time
2. The Scale System™ complete module - Phase 3 of the method: hiring overseas editors ($500-$1,200/mo), building SOPs, quality control, client retention playbooks
3. The Streamer Niche Playbook Library - pre-built playbooks for 5 streaming verticals (gaming, Just Chatting, music, IRL, VTubing)
4. Done-with-you first client acquisition - a coach walks the member through the first 10 outreach messages, reviews proposals, helps close
5. Monthly performance reviews - submit agency metrics, get personalized strategic feedback
6. Accountability partner matching - weekly check-ins with another member at the same level

**Anchor:** $12,800 (12 coaching sessions at $500 each = $6K + course value $4,200 + playbooks $1,500 + accountability $1,100)
**Timeline to Results:** First client within 21 days. 3-4 clients within 60 days. $8K-$12K/month within 90 days.

---

### Tier 3: Clip Partner (DFY) - $7,500

1-on-1 coaching + done-for-you setup + ongoing strategic partnership. 6 months of 1-on-1 support.

**Deliverables (everything in Tier 2, plus):**
1. Bi-weekly 1-on-1 coaching calls (12 sessions over 6 months)
2. Done-for-you agency setup - website/portfolio, CRM configuration, custom outreach sequences, project management system
3. First 3 clients sourced - team identifies and warms up 20+ potential streamer clients, helps close the first 3
4. Editor recruitment assistance - vetting and onboarding the first 2 editors from a curated editor network
5. Private Slack/Discord channel - direct access to the founding team

**Anchor:** $33,000 (12 coaching sessions at $1,500/each = $18K + DFY setup $5K + client sourcing $5K + editor recruitment $3K + private access $2K)
**Timeline to Results:** First client within 14 days. $10K/month within 60 days. $15K-$20K/month within 90 days.

---

**Bonus Stack (applies across tiers):**
- **Bonus 1 - The Streamer Outreach Swipe File ($497 value).** Solves the #1 pain: "I don't know what to say when I DM a streamer." 25 scripts organized by streamer size, platform, niche.
- **Bonus 2 - The AI Clip Production Playbook ($297 value).** Solves: "I'm slow at editing." Complete setup for the AI+human hybrid workflow.
- **Bonus 3 - The Agency Finance Dashboard ($197 value).** Solves: "I don't know if my agency is profitable." Pre-built spreadsheet tracking revenue, editor costs, margins, MRR forecasting.
- **Bonus 4 - The Client Retention Playbook ($397 value).** Solves: "I keep losing clients after 2-3 months." Monthly reporting templates + the Sticky Client System.

**Guarantee (Conditional Performance):** "Complete all 8 course modules, attend at least 8 of 12 coaching calls, send at least 50 outreach messages using our scripts - and if you don't land at least 1 paying client within 90 days, we give you an additional 90 days of coaching and community free, plus a 1-on-1 strategy session to diagnose what's not working."

**Urgency (real):** Capacity-limited cohorts at 25 members because group coaching requires live hot-seats. Above 25, quality degrades. New cohorts open quarterly.

**Economics Validation:**
- Blended LTV: $3,448 (50% Tier 2, 30% Tier 1, 20% Tier 3)
- Blended CAC: ~$500 (60% organic, 40% paid)
- **LTV:CAC 6.9:1** - well above the 3:1 minimum
- Gross margin per Tier 2 student: 63%

## Why This Is A Good Example

- **Shows the offer architect's tiered pricing ratio in the wild.** 1X / 3X / 7.5X across $997 / $2,997 / $7,500 - close to the documented 1X / 2X / 4-5X but adjusted upward for a premium-low-margin-high category. The psychology is assumption-close (which tier, not whether).
- **Target tier positioning is explicit.** The offer document names Tier 2 as TARGET TIER because that's where the math works - LTV:CAC, margin, and fulfillment capacity all peak at the DWY tier. Tier 1 is a lead-feeder; Tier 3 is a capacity-constrained upsell.
- **Bonuses target named objections.** Each of the four bonuses is mapped to a specific pain point from the ICP document (outreach scripts → Pain 1 "can't find clients"; finance dashboard → ICP Section 6 Pain 3 "stuck at freelancer income"). This is the acquisition economist's Step 10 discipline applied - no bonus exists without a problem-solved slot.
