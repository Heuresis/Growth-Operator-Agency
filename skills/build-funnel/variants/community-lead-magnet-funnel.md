# Community Lead Magnet Funnel — Archetype Playbook

> **When it wins:** Skool / Circle / Discord / Slack / Facebook Group-based businesses where the community IS the lead magnet + delivery vehicle + retention mechanism. Continuity + ascension as the monetization spine.

## When to pick this archetype

- Offer model is a community (monthly / annual subscription OR lifetime access to community + content).
- Price ladder: free tier → paid tier ($37–$297/mo OR $997–$5K lifetime).
- You value retention / engagement / peer-to-peer value over sales-team intensity.
- ICP wants access to peers + creator, not just content.
- You can sustain weekly / monthly content + event cadence in the community.

## The 5-stage structure

### 1. Awareness

- **Traffic:** content (creator posts), word-of-mouth, paid amplification, referrals from existing members.
- **Entry point:** "Join the free community" CTA on content, sales page, referral page.

### 2. Interest

- **Free community:** open or low-barrier. Email opt-in typical. Discord / free Skool / Facebook Group.
- **Onboarding experience:** welcome post, DM from creator or community manager, first-week engagement prompts.
- **CVR benchmark:** free-community opt-in rate 20–40% on landing pages.

### 3. Consideration

- **In-community value delivery:**
  - Weekly / monthly host content (lives, Q&As, teardowns, AMAs)
  - Peer-to-peer posts + comments
  - Featured members + success showcases
  - Structured resources / vault / archive

- **Nurture mechanism:** the community itself — members consume, engage, see success, and organically start considering the paid tier.

### 4. Decision

- **Ascension triggers:**
  - Paid tier announcement in community
  - Limited-time pricing or bonus window
  - 1:1 DM from creator to high-engagement members
  - Annual open-cart window

- **Paid-tier offer:** structured differently based on model:
  - Monthly subscription (low friction, higher churn risk)
  - Annual (higher commitment, lower churn, CAC payback in 6–12 months)
  - Lifetime ($997–$5K, no recurring, all-in commitment)

- **Close rate:** 2–8% of free-community members ascend within 90 days on a well-run community; 10–20% over 12 months.

### 5. Action

- **Paid community onboarding:** welcome, 7-day orientation, first-win engineering.
- **Retention:** month-1, month-3, month-6, month-12 milestone interventions.
- **Referral loop:** paid members invite prospects → referral incentive → free-tier growth.

## CVR benchmarks

| Stage | Monthly model | Annual / Lifetime |
|---|---|---|
| Traffic → free community | 20–40% | 20–40% |
| Free → paid (30-day) | 0.5–3% | 1–4% |
| Free → paid (12-month) | 5–15% | 10–20% |
| Paid monthly retention (month-3) | 75–88% | 85–95% (annual) |
| Paid retention (month-12) | 30–55% | 60–80% (annual) |

## Tech stack

- **Community platform:** Skool / Circle / Discord / Slack / Facebook Groups / Mighty Networks
- **Free-to-paid gate:** platform-native or Stripe subscription + conditional access
- **Email:** ConvertKit / ActiveCampaign (community broadcasts)
- **Retention tracking:** platform analytics + weekly cohort review
- **Referral system:** platform-native or Rewardful / First Promoter

## Creative requirements

- **Content cadence in the community:** weekly live + daily asynchronous host presence for first 90 days of a new community.
- **Ascension emails / DMs:** 3–5 per month to free-tier members.
- **Success stories:** 1 featured member per week.
- **Paid-tier sales page:** a single page articulating what the paid tier unlocks.

## Economics

- **LTV math (monthly model):** avg LTV = monthly price × avg months retained. Target 8–15 months average retention.
- **LTV math (annual / lifetime):** price ÷ churn rate (annual model) OR gross margin on lifetime sale.
- **CAC tolerance:** typically lower than high-ticket funnels because ascension is slow; target CAC ≤ 3 months of paid revenue.

## Common failure modes

1. **Free community becomes support / complaint forum.** Fix: explicit community guidelines + moderation SOP + "asks go in designated thread."
2. **No host presence in first 90 days of new community.** Fix: creator commits to 30 min / day engagement for first 90 days; non-negotiable.
3. **Paid tier not differentiated from free.** Members don't see why to ascend. Fix: paid tier locks specific content, events, access, or resources that free tier can see but can't access.
4. **Churn in month-2 of paid tier.** Fix: onboarding sequence (first-win + community intro + creator DM) in weeks 1–2.
5. **Referral / growth stalls at 100–500 members.** Fix: referral program + partnership recruits + content export from community-only content.
6. **Community platform migration pain.** Facebook → Skool / Discord → Circle, etc. Fix: pick platform once, commit. Migrating fractures the community.

## Variant sub-archetypes

- **Free community → paid community:** default pattern.
- **Free community → paid course + community access:** course is the main asset, community is the bonus.
- **Free community → paid mastermind / paid 1:1:** community is the top-of-funnel for higher-ticket offers.
- **Paid-only community (no free tier):** smaller, premium. Typically $497+/mo or $3K+/year.
