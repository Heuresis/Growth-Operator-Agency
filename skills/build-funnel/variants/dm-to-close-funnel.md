# DM-to-Close Funnel — Archetype Playbook

> **When it wins:** Founder-led sales. Small team. High-trust category where the buyer decides based on the relationship, not the funnel. Social-first creators with 10K–250K engaged followers.

## When to pick this archetype

- Creator brand carries the trust; prospects arrive primed.
- You're founder-led or have 1–2 closers max.
- Volume target: 5–20 closes per week (at $5K–$25K).
- ICP responds to content + human conversation, not automated sequences.
- You have (or are building) high-signal content — DMs open because the content earned the right.
- Niche where trust matters more than funnel efficiency (consulting, high-ticket coaching, creative services, investors).

## The 5-stage structure

### 1. Awareness

- **Traffic:** 100% organic content (LinkedIn, X, YouTube, podcast, Instagram). No paid ads typically.
- **Content pillar:** 2–4 pieces per week that demonstrate expertise.
- **Signal:** prospects engage (likes, comments, DMs, replies).

### 2. Interest

- **DM trigger:** prospect DMs the creator directly ("how do you work with people?") OR creator DMs the prospect based on public engagement.
- **DM opener (creator-initiated):** specific + referenced (not "hey, let's connect"). `prompts/email-prompts.md` Prompt 2 for cold-email logic applies.
- **Inbound DM response (creator-side):** respond within 24 hours, conversational, qualify in 2–3 back-and-forth messages.

### 3. Consideration

- **DM qualification (2–5 messages):**
  - What they want help with (specific)
  - Current stage / context
  - Timeline / urgency
  - Whether they're the decision-maker

- **If qualified:** move to a call.
- **If not qualified:** route to content, lower-tier offer, or politely close ("here's a resource that might help; let's stay in touch").

### 4. Decision

- **Call:** 30–60 min discovery + pitch. Conversational, not scripted (prospect already primed by content).
- **Close rate on qualified DM-to-call:** 25–50% (high because self-qualified prospects).
- **On-call payment / deposit:** preferred.

### 5. Action

- **Post-close:** contract + payment + onboarding.
- **Non-closer follow-up:** keep in the content orbit; DM check-in at 30 / 60 days if fit still exists.

## CVR benchmarks

| Stage | Good | Great | Elite |
|---|---|---|---|
| Content → DM trigger | 0.5% | 1.5% | 3%+ |
| DM → call booked | 25–40% | 40–60% | 60–75% |
| Call booked → show | 85–95% | 90–95% | 95%+ |
| Call show → close | 25–40% | 40–55% | 55–70% |
| Blended engaged-audience → close | 0.1% | 0.5% | 1.5%+ |

## Tech stack

- **Content:** LinkedIn / X / YouTube / Instagram native
- **DM:** platform-native (no third-party DM automation for high-ticket — kills trust)
- **CRM:** Close / HubSpot / Pipedrive / simple spreadsheet (low-volume)
- **Scheduler:** Calendly / SavvyCal
- **Call recording:** Zoom + Fathom / Fireflies
- **Payment:** Stripe Payment Links

## Creative requirements

- **Content production:** sustained weekly — the content IS the funnel.
- **DM templates:** 3–5 opener variants for inbound + outbound; not copy-paste.
- **Call-brief process:** `skills/call-prep/SKILL.md` on every call.
- **Follow-up templates:** simple 3-touch DM follow-up for non-closers.

## Economics

- **CAC per close:** near-zero (content is the acquisition).
- **CAC per content hour:** creator's time + any production team cost.
- **LTV:CAC:** irrelevant in the classic sense; the metric is closes-per-week × average deal size vs creator + team hours.
- **Scaling ceiling:** maxes out at 5–20 closes per week per creator. Beyond that, transition to application funnel.

## Common failure modes

1. **Content doesn't attract ICP.** Prospects DM but none are fit. Fix: audit content → does it attract the right buyer or the wrong one?
2. **DM response too slow.** 48+ hour response → prospect cools. Fix: same-day response protocol.
3. **Automation creeps in.** Canned DM replies get sniffed out. Fix: human response only, even if short.
4. **No qualification before call.** Unqualified calls waste creator time. Fix: 2–5 message qualification in DM first.
5. **Creator burnout.** DM-to-close scales linearly with creator hours. Fix: know when to transition to application funnel (around 20 closes/week).
6. **No CRM discipline.** Prospects fall through. Fix: simple pipeline tracking.

## Variant sub-archetypes

- **Pure inbound DM:** prospects DM creator. Best signal-to-effort.
- **Outbound DM after engagement:** creator DMs people who engaged with a specific post.
- **Cold DM outreach:** creator DMs targeted prospects proactively. Lower conversion; only works when offer is high-fit for the recipient.
- **Voice-DM variant:** creator sends 30–60 sec voice notes instead of text DMs. Higher response rate in some niches.
