# Book / Free+Shipping Funnel — Archetype Playbook

> **When it wins:** Authority-first positioning. Long sales cycle with high-touch sales team. Backend monetization through ascension (consulting, mastermind, event). The book is the lead magnet; the backend is the business.

## When to pick this archetype

- Price ladder: book (free+shipping $5.95 OR $17–$27) → consult / workshop ($2K–$10K) → mastermind / high-ticket ($10K–$100K+).
- You have a book (or are about to publish one). Or a bound multi-chapter resource that feels book-like.
- ICP respects authors + books more than video content (executives, founders, physicians, investors).
- You have sales capacity to handle application-funnel scale on backend.
- Event / workshop / mastermind is part of the offer ladder.

## The 5-stage structure

### 1. Awareness

- **Traffic:** paid (Meta / Google / LinkedIn for B2B), organic (author platform, podcast tour), referrals, PR.
- **Ad creative:** "Get my book for free — just pay shipping." OR "Buy my book for $17 + bonus chapters."
- **Land:** book sales page (hero image of book + 3-chapter summary + order bump + checkout).

### 2. Interest

- **Order form:** minimal — name, email, shipping address, payment.
- **Order bump:** audio version of book ($17–$47) or bonus workbook ($10–$27). Take rate 30–60%.
- **Upsell #1 (one-click):** book-related training ($97–$297). Take rate 10–20%.
- **Upsell #2:** consultation / workshop offer ($497–$2K). Take rate 3–8%.

### 3. Consideration

- **Post-sale (immediate):**
  - Digital book / audio / workbook delivered via email
  - Physical book ships within 48 hours
  - Welcome email + reading plan / first 3 chapters orientation

- **Reading sequence (14–30 days):**
  - 3–5 emails that accompany the book as reader engages with it
  - Each email expands on one chapter + points to the backend

### 4. Decision

- **Backend ascension path (weeks 2–12 after book purchase):**
  - **Option A:** webinar invite → cohort offer
  - **Option B:** book-a-call CTA → consulting or group offer
  - **Option C:** event / mastermind invite → direct enrollment
  - **Option D:** application → high-ticket 1:1 or mastermind

- **Close rate on book-buyer backend:** 3–8% ascend to $2K+ offer within 90 days; 10–20% over 12 months.

### 5. Action

- **Post-ascension:** standard onboarding for the purchased offer.
- **Long-nurture for non-ascenders:** quarterly broadcasts, annual launches, content-led warming.
- **Reader-community invitation:** free community for book readers, natural content-led ascension over time.

## Tech stack

- **Fulfillment:** Book print + ship via Amazon KDP / IngramSpark / Kindle Direct or third-party fulfillment (ShipBob / ShipMonk).
- **Funnel / checkout:** ClickFunnels-class / SamCart / ThriveCart.
- **Shipping calculator:** platform-native; real cost + small margin.
- **Email + CRM:** ActiveCampaign / Klaviyo / Customer.io.
- **Backend offer funnel:** whichever fits — webinar, application, book-a-call.

## Creative requirements

- **Book cover + mockup:** professional design non-negotiable.
- **Ad creative:** lead with the book's promise, not the backend. 15+ variants in rotation.
- **Book-buyer email sequence:** 5–10 emails over 30 days.
- **Backend offer collateral:** VSL / webinar / sales page / application form.

## Economics

- **Direct book economics typically break even or lose money:** $17 revenue − $8 fulfillment − $15 CAC = -$6 per book.
- **Order bump + upsells turn book-front into profit:** AOV of $40+ at $15 CAC = $25 profit per buyer.
- **The real economics are backend:** $400–$1,200 LTV per book-buyer over 12 months drives the model.
- **LTV:CAC target:** 4:1+ (backend-heavy).

## Common failure modes

1. **Book quality suspect.** Readers don't finish → don't ascend. Fix: genuinely valuable book, not a lead-magnet disguised as one.
2. **No bumps / upsells.** Front-end loses money, backend doesn't pick up. Fix: add bump ($17–$47) + upsell ($97–$297).
3. **No backend offer ready.** Book buyers wait, forget, go cold. Fix: have the backend offer live before the book launches.
4. **Shipping delays kill engagement.** Fix: 48-hour ship; digital delivery immediate.
5. **Book traffic costs don't scale.** Physical product + CAC squeeze margin. Fix: use the book primarily to build the list + brand; measure success on 90-day backend ascension, not book P&L.
6. **Book sold to wrong audience.** Generic readers don't fit backend ICP. Fix: tighten ad targeting to ICP; let freebie-seekers pass.

## Variant sub-archetypes

- **Free + shipping ($5.95–$9.95):** lowest commitment, highest volume, broadest audience.
- **Paid $7–$27:** filters better, same book content, 2–3× higher ascension rate.
- **Paid $47–$97 (bundled with bonus course):** the book + extras model. Mid-ticket front-end.
- **Book-a-call-after-book:** every book buyer gets a personal outreach DM within 7 days. High-touch, low-scale, highest backend close rate.
- **Event-anchored book:** book buyers invited to $297–$997 in-person or virtual event → mastermind enrollment there.
