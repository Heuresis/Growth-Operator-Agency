# Multi-Video Pre-Launch Runway Funnel — Archetype Playbook

> **When it wins:** Cohort launches with a natural education arc. 3–5 video teaching sequence warms cold-to-medium traffic before a cart-open window. Generic pattern inspired by the 3-video pre-launch runway — works for any market that needs belief installation before ask.

## When to pick this archetype

- You run cohort launches (2–4 per year) with fixed cart-open + cart-close dates.
- Offer price $997–$10K, cohort-delivered.
- ICP benefits from 7–14 days of pre-launch education before the offer ask.
- You have (or are building) an email list + social audience that can consume the pre-launch content.
- You can produce 3–5 polished videos (15–30 min each) timed over a 1–2 week runway.

## The 5-stage structure

### 1. Awareness (pre-runway, weeks -4 to -1)

- **Traffic build:** paid + organic + referrals. Runway opens with the pre-launch email list.
- **Pre-runway opt-in:** "Get the free training series starting [date]." Lead-magnet style.
- **Teaser content:** 1–2 weeks of social content building anticipation.

### 2. Interest (runway starts — Video 1)

- **Video 1 "The What / Promise":** broad reframe of the problem. Shows the viewer what's possible + introduces the method category. Doesn't pitch.
- **Duration:** 15–30 min.
- **Delivered:** email (with video embed or unlisted URL) + optional community post.
- **Engagement target:** 40–60% of registrants watch at least 50% of Video 1.

### 3. Consideration (Videos 2–3)

- **Video 2 "The How / Transformation":** teaches the core mechanism. Case study or demonstration. Installs belief that the method works.
- **Video 3 "The What If / Ownership":** shows what the prospect's life / business looks like AFTER implementing. Bridge to the offer.

- **Between videos:** engagement posts, Q&A / DM engagement, community thread, comment replies. Prospect feels like they're in a live event.

### 4. Decision (runway concludes — Video 4 OR cart-open event)

- **Video 4 / cart-open webinar (cart-open day):** the offer reveal. 60–90 min webinar OR a polished offer video.
  - Restate the journey
  - Offer introduction (core + bonuses + guarantee)
  - Stack slide + price reveal
  - Urgency: cohort seat cap + cart-close timestamp

- **Cart window:** 3–7 days.
- **Cart-sequence emails:** 5–10 emails, each addressing one top objection.

### 5. Action

- **Post-purchase onboarding:** standard.
- **Non-closer follow-up:** wait-list for next cohort + optional lower-tier offer / continuity.

## CVR benchmarks

| Stage | Good | Great | Elite |
|---|---|---|---|
| Opt-in to runway | 20–40% | 40–55% | 55–70% |
| Video 1 engagement | 40% | 55% | 70%+ |
| Video 1 → Video 3 attrition | 30–50% | 20–35% | 10–20% |
| Cart-open attendance | 30–50% | 45–60% | 60–75% |
| Cart-open attendance → close | 5–15% | 15–25% | 25–40% |
| Blended opt-in → close | 2–5% | 5–12% | 12–25% |

## Tech stack

- **Video hosting:** Wistia / Vimeo / Video.js (hosted control — don't use YouTube for paid-launch videos).
- **Email + automation:** ActiveCampaign / ConvertKit / Klaviyo.
- **Runway page:** a dedicated "vault" page that unlocks videos by day.
- **Engagement tracking:** per-video watch-time; triggers segmentation (engaged vs at-risk).
- **Community / DMs:** Slack / Discord / Skool / FB Group for live engagement during runway.

## Creative requirements

- **3–4 pre-launch videos:** each 15–30 min, production-quality, consistent visual.
- **Cart-open event video / webinar:** 45–90 min.
- **Pre-runway nurture:** 3–5 emails before runway starts.
- **Runway emails:** daily or every-other-day, each reinforcing the video.
- **Cart-open sequence:** 5–10 emails during 3–7 day cart window.

## Economics

- **CAC on runway traffic:** mid-high — cold-traffic opt-in → runway-watch → close ladder is long.
- **Close concentration:** 60–80% of closes happen on cart-open-day or last-2-days. Run the numbers across the whole window.
- **LTV:CAC target:** 3:1 blended.
- **Cohort economics:** 500 runway-enrollees × 10% close × $2K = $100K cohort; if CAC per enrollee is $80, CAC total = $40K. LTV:CAC = 2.5:1 (borderline; improve by lifting close rate or raising price).

## Common failure modes

1. **Videos too sales-y too early.** Video 1 pitches → list tunes out. Fix: Video 1 is teaching; pitching starts Video 4.
2. **Gaps between videos too long.** 7+ days between V1 and V2 kills momentum. Fix: max 3–4 days between videos.
3. **No engagement mechanism.** Runway feels like one-way broadcast. Fix: comments + DMs + community threads daily.
4. **Cart-open webinar conflicts with runway pace.** Abrupt tonal shift. Fix: Video 4 / cart-open feels like the natural conclusion of V1–V3.
5. **Cart window too long.** 10+ days → attendance drift + close rate drops. Fix: 3–5 days typical; 7 days max.
6. **No wait-list for non-closers.** Next cohort re-acquires. Fix: wait-list sequence + early-bird for next cohort.

## Variant sub-archetypes

- **3-video runway (7-day) + live open:** default. Most operators use this.
- **5-video runway (14-day):** deeper belief installation, better for complex / expensive offers.
- **7-video runway (21-day):** rare; works for very high-ticket ($10K+) with serious sales complexity.
- **Daily micro-runway (7-day):** 7 short videos (5–10 min each) instead of 3–4 long. Good for impatient audiences.
