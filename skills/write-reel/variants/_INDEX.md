## Decision Tree

```
IF pillar is Case Study OR Proof AND verifiable number exists:
    → Money Reveal (1) or Case Study Breakdown (6)
ELIF pillar is Transformation AND two states documented:
    → Before/After (7)
ELIF pillar is Capability AND artifact builds in under 5 min:
    → Speed Build (2)
ELIF pillar is Positioning AND real difference exists:
    → Comparison (3)
ELIF pillar is Myth-Bust AND creator holds defensible counter-position:
    → Contrarian Take (4)
ELIF pillar is Lifestyle AND creator's day has distinct shape:
    → Day in the Life (8)
ELIF pillar is Trust-building AND creator comfortable with raw footage:
    → Behind the Scenes (5)
ELIF pillar is Personal Narrative AND mistake is 6+ months past:
    → Mistake / Lesson (9)
ELIF pillar is Service AND real question exists:
    → Question Answered (10)
ELSE:
    → Default to Question Answered (10) — broadest applicability, lowest risk
```

## Cross-Variant Invariants

All 10 variants enforce:

- Hook Specificity ≥ 8 (per `reference/frameworks/primitives/specificity.md`)
- 60-second runtime ceiling
- Voice-match (3+ `phrases_to_use` from Compartment 1)
- Zero items from `spec/BANNED-VOCABULARY.md`
- One CTA or one loop, not both
- Silent-viewable — text overlay communicates the payoff without audio
- Platform adaptations specified for Instagram, TikTok, YouTube Shorts
- Compliance check (no income guarantees, no fabrication, FTC disclosures where applicable)

## Failure Modes Common Across Variants

1. Hook specificity below 8 — generic opener kills reach in the first two seconds.
2. Missing proof — claims without on-screen artifact read as fabrication.
3. Voice drift — creator voice replaced by "AI-generated informative" cadence.
4. No loop and no CTA — reel ends without a next-step for the viewer.
5. Platform-agnostic caption — same caption used everywhere, no adaptation.
6. Banned vocabulary leakage — one "unlock" or "transform" verb fails the compliance pass.
7. Runtime overrun — 60+ seconds kills IG / TikTok algorithmic weighting.

## Re-Engage Cadence (All Variants)

- T+0 to T+90 min: creator actively replies to the first 20 comments.
- T+24h: pinned comment extending the reel with one more specific detail.
- T+48h: quote-repost or follow-up reel on the best-performing angle.
- T+7d: long-form asset (YouTube or newsletter) expanding the reel into depth.

## Hand-off to Editor (Universal)

Every reel shipped to editing includes:

- Scene-by-scene shot list with timestamps
- Text overlays with exact copy and duration
- B-roll requirements
- Music / audio direction
- Loop vs CTA decision
- Platform adaptations for IG, TikTok, YouTube Shorts
- Compliance check signed off
