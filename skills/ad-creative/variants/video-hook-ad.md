# Video Hook Ad — Archetype Playbook

> **When it wins:** Cold feed ads where the first 3 seconds decide everything. Short-form (15–45 sec). The hook IS the ad's conversion; body only has to hold retention.

## When to pick this ad type

- Running paid ads on Meta / TikTok / YouTube Shorts cold feeds.
- You have 20+ hook variants to test (you will burn through 5–10 per week).
- You're optimizing CAC → looking for cheap cold impressions that convert.
- Your offer has an audience reachable via broad interest targeting.

## Structure

### Hook (0:00–0:03)
- **The most important 3 seconds in paid acquisition.**
- Options:
  - **Pattern interrupt:** unexpected visual / sound / motion
  - **Direct address:** "If you're a [X], watch this"
  - **Stat / claim:** "I spent $847 and made $21,000. Here's how."
  - **Contrarian hook:** "Everyone says [common advice]. I disagree. Here's why."
  - **Identity match:** "This is for [specific subset]"

- Must be the first thing said + first thing shown
- No "hey guys" / "in this video" / "welcome back"

### Body (0:03–0:25)
- Immediately pay off the hook's promise
- 1–2 specific teaching / proof beats
- Maintain visual motion (cuts every 2–3 sec)

### Close (0:25–0:40)
- Explicit CTA: "Link below / click the link in bio / swipe up"
- Brief urgency if real
- URL on-screen if possible

## Hook variants to test

See `prompts/ad-creative-prompts.md` Prompt 1 for a 30-hook grid. Key patterns:

- Question hooks: *"What if I told you [unexpected claim]?"*
- Contrarian hooks: *"Everyone's wrong about [common belief]"*
- Stat hooks: *"88% of [ICP] never [specific action]"*
- Identity hooks: *"If you're a [ICP] making under $X, this is for you"*
- Callout hooks: *"Stop scrolling if you're tired of [specific thing]"*
- Story hooks: *"Three months ago I was [low point]. Today [high point]. Here's what changed."*

## Creative requirements

- Native aspect ratio (9:16 for Reels/Shorts/TikTok, 1:1 or 4:5 for feed)
- Captions burned-in (80%+ of viewers have sound off)
- No intro bumper, no logo splash for first 3 seconds
- Direct audio (not music-first)

## CVR benchmarks

| Metric | Good | Great | Elite |
|---|---|---|---|
| 3-second video retention | 55% | 70% | 85%+ |
| CTR (to landing page) | 0.6% | 1.5% | 3.0%+ |
| Cost per click | $0.70–$3 | $0.30–$1.50 | $0.15–$0.80 |

## Common failure modes

1. **Slow opener.** 3-second retention below 40%. Fix: cut everything before the first claim.
2. **Hook promises more than body delivers.** CTR high, conversion low. Fix: hook must be what the video delivers.
3. **Creative fatigue at 7–14 days.** Fix: 3–5 new hook variants weekly.
4. **Platform mismatch.** Meta creative run on TikTok underperforms. Fix: platform-specific edits.
5. **No platform-native elements.** Feels like an ad, not a post. Fix: captions, texture, pacing matching platform.

## Testing discipline

- Launch 5–10 hook variants simultaneously at small budget ($20–$50 each)
- Let each hit ≥ 1,000 impressions before judging
- Winners (top 20% by CTR + cost/conversion) → scale
- Rest → pause + new variants
- Weekly cadence: 3–5 new hooks produced + tested
