| [interview-ad.md](interview-ad.md) | Interview-style | Conversational authority; longer-form engagement |

## Cross-ad-type rules

- **Hook in first 3 seconds** (all video) or **three-word scan** (all static).
- **One CTA per ad** — multiple CTAs dilute.
- **Compliance baseline** — no fabricated results, no medical claims without disclosure, no income claims without FTC-compliant disclaimers.
- **15–30 variants per type in rotation** at scale; fatigue appears at 500K–1M impressions per variant.
