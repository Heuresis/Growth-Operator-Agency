# Retargeting Ad — Archetype Playbook

> **When it wins:** Warm traffic that engaged but didn't convert. Second / third / nth touch. Lower CPM, higher CVR. Often 50–200% of total ad-driven revenue at scale.

## When to pick this ad type

- You already have cold-traffic ads running.
- Pixel is collecting: page-viewers, VSL-watchers, add-to-cart abandoners, lead-opt-ins-who-didn't-book.
- Your offer has a 7+ day consideration window.
- You have assets for specific-objection reframes (not just recycling the cold-ad creative).

## Audience segmentation

Retargeting buckets + their correct next-touch:

| Segment | Warmth | Best ad message |
|---|---|---|
| Visited VSL page, didn't watch | Cool | Re-intro with new hook |
| Watched 25–75% of VSL | Warm | Objection-reframe (pick the likely drop-off objection) |
| Watched 75%+ of VSL | Hot | Specific CTA / bonus / scarcity |
| Opted in, didn't book | Hot | Specific show-up / booking incentive |
| Booked, didn't show | Hot | Show-rate reminder (if timing right) |
| Abandoned cart | Very hot | One-time bonus / guarantee reminder |
| Customer (past buyer) | Repeat-possible | Ascension offer, next-tier |

## Structure (15–45 seconds)

### Hook (0:00–0:03)
- Acknowledge the prior touch naturally: "You watched my training on X..." (without being creepy)
- Or: lead with the specific pain / reframe the prospect was stuck on

### Body (0:03–0:30)
- ONE objection-reframe per ad
- OR: new proof not shown in cold ad
- OR: bonus / scarcity / guarantee reminder

### CTA (0:30–0:45)
- Hard-direct: "Book your call" / "Claim your spot" / "Get it today"
- URL clear; landing alignment-tight (matches ad copy)

## Creative requirements

- Different tone from cold ad — not a re-run
- Can be more direct-response than cold creative
- Face-to-camera often outperforms voiceover for retargeting (trust signal)
- Platform-native format

## Cadence + frequency caps

- Frequency cap per unique: 3–5 per 7 days. Over that, the prospect feels stalked.
- 14-day retargeting window for most offers; 30-day for high-ticket.
- 3–5 creative variants per audience segment in rotation.

## CVR benchmarks

| Metric | Cold baseline | Retargeting |
|---|---|---|
| CTR | 1–2% | 3–8% |
| Cost per click | $2–$8 | $0.80–$3 |
| Cost per conversion | $50–$200 | $15–$60 |
| Revenue per retargeting dollar | 2–4× cold | 5–12× cold |

## Common failure modes

1. **Same creative as cold ad.** Prospect saw it already. Fix: distinct creative, distinct angle.
2. **No segmentation.** Everyone gets the same ad. Fix: 3–5 retargeting segments each with own creative.
3. **Over-frequency.** Prospect blocked / annoyed. Fix: frequency caps.
4. **Landing-page mismatch.** Ad says X, page says Y. Fix: tight ad-to-page alignment.
5. **No win-back sequence.** Retargeting ends at day 14; buyer never re-approached. Fix: longer-term orbit strategy (email nurture, occasional re-retarget).
