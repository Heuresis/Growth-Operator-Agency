# Profile Funnel Ad — Archetype Playbook

> **When it wins:** Low-CPM cold traffic acquisition. Organic-feeling post becomes an ad by adding paid reach. Feed-native vs production-heavy.

## When to pick this ad type

- You want to scale cold traffic affordably.
- Your creator brand has authenticity as an asset — feed-native ads extend that.
- Your platform is one where organic content and ads blur (Instagram, TikTok, LinkedIn).
- You have 3–10 high-performing organic posts to promote as ads.
- You value CAC-per-click efficiency over click-through spike.

## Structure (15–45 seconds typical)

### Hook (0:00–0:03)
- Feels like a post, not an ad
- No logo splash, no polish intro
- Direct-to-camera OR direct-to-text
- Specific claim or question

### Body (0:03–0:25)
- One clear insight / teaching / story
- Delivered conversationally
- No sales pitch tone
- Single actionable takeaway

### CTA (0:25–0:45)
- Soft: "If you want more on this, link in bio / in comments / swipe up"
- Or: "I'm hosting a [free thing] on [date] — link below"
- NOT hard direct-response: "Buy now" / "Limited time"

## Creative requirements

- Native aspect ratio (9:16 vertical for IG/TikTok Reels; 1:1 or 4:5 for feed; 16:9 for YouTube)
- Simple captions (built-in or via platform tool — critical for mobile-sound-off)
- No heavy brand elements (logo, watermark) unless minimal
- Creator-centric: the creator's face or voice is present

## Variants to test

- **Creator POV:** direct-to-camera, phone held in hand feel
- **On-location:** recorded in context (office, gym, coffee shop — relevant to offer)
- **Text-over-image:** single frame with text overlay (works on TikTok/Reels)
- **Screen-record + voiceover:** "let me show you something" format

## CVR benchmarks

| Metric | Good | Great |
|---|---|---|
| CTR (to lead magnet / landing) | 0.8% | 2.0%+ |
| Cost per click | $0.50–$2.50 | $0.30–$1.20 |
| Cost per lead | $3–$15 | $1.50–$6 |
| Cost per high-ticket call book | $80–$250 | $40–$150 |

## Common failure modes

1. **Too polished → reads as ad.** Fix: keep production intentionally low-fi.
2. **Hard-sell tone.** Fix: soft CTA, value-first body.
3. **No clear insight.** "Hey, I'm Y and I help X" doesn't earn attention. Fix: deliver one specific insight.
4. **Platform-mismatched aspect ratio.** 16:9 on Reels feed = scroll-past. Fix: native aspect ratio per platform.

## Platforms + specific notes

- **Instagram / Facebook:** Reels as ads is typical; Stories ads work but lower CTR.
- **TikTok:** hook ads shortest (3-sec kill); organic-feel is mandatory.
- **LinkedIn:** longer-form conversational (30–60 sec); B2B operators perform best.
- **YouTube Shorts ads:** native Shorts rarely beat long-form YouTube ads for offer conversion; use for reach, not conversion.
