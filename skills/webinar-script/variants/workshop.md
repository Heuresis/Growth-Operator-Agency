# Premium Workshop — Format Playbook

> **When it wins:** Paid attendance ($47–$997) filters for serious buyers. 90–180 min of deep teach + mid-ticket or high-ticket upsell at the end. Smaller attendee count but dramatically higher close rate per attendee.

## When to pick this format over a free webinar

- You have the authority to charge $47–$997 for a workshop.
- Offer laddering: workshop → $2K–$25K backend.
- Want to filter out freebie-seekers; skew attendee base toward buyers.
- Teach-heavy content that earns the price — not just a pitch in disguise.
- Can deliver 90–180 min of genuine value (not filler).

## Structure (180-min example; compress for shorter)

### 0:00–0:20 — Welcome + expectation-setting
- Welcome + host intro
- "What you'll walk away with" (specific outcomes)
- Housekeeping (Q&A, breaks, recording, worksheet)
- Brief credibility injection (not hero-worship)
- First teaching beat starts by 0:15 — don't lose them in intro

### 0:20–1:00 — Core Teaching Segment 1 (Foundation)
- Biggest reframe the attendee needs to internalize
- 3–5 teaching points, each with one example / case study
- Assignment / reflection prompt (2–5 min)
- Brief Q&A cluster (5–10 min)

### 1:00–1:10 — Break

### 1:10–1:50 — Core Teaching Segment 2 (Mechanism)
- Teach the mechanism in depth (not a surface sketch)
- Live demo / worked example if possible
- 2 additional teaching points
- Assignment / practice (5–10 min)
- Q&A cluster (10 min)

### 1:50–2:00 — Break

### 2:00–2:40 — Core Teaching Segment 3 (Implementation)
- How attendees apply the mechanism in the next 7/30/90 days
- The 3 biggest implementation failures + how to avoid them
- Case study of successful implementation (someone specific)
- Assignment / action step for after workshop

### 2:40–3:00 — Ownership + Transition + Offer (the close)
- "What's next if you want to go deeper"
- Transition from teaching tone to offer tone — acknowledge the shift explicitly
- Offer reveal: core + bonuses + guarantee
- Stack slide + price reveal
- Urgency (cohort start, bonus expiration, or workshop-attendee-only pricing)
- Close + CTA
- Final Q&A (10 min)

## Attendee experience

### Before workshop
- Registration with payment ($47–$997)
- Confirmation email + calendar + pre-work (optional light reading, 15–30 min)
- 3-email sequence over 7 days before:
  - Day -7: welcome + preview
  - Day -3: reminder + what to prepare
  - Day -1: final reminder + Zoom link

### During workshop
- Worksheet / workbook provided (real, usable)
- Screen share with slides + occasional split-screen to host
- Active Q&A (text chat + timed voice Q&A)
- Breaks announced + respected (180-min workshop needs 2 × 10-min breaks)

### After workshop
- Recording delivered within 48 hours
- Workbook solutions / reference delivered
- Follow-up sequence: 3 emails over 7 days
  - Day +1: thank-you + recording + recap
  - Day +3: "did you try the assignment?" + proof-story
  - Day +7: last-chance reminder on backend offer (if workshop-attendee-only pricing)

## Close rate benchmarks

| Segment | Good | Great | Elite |
|---|---|---|---|
| Registration → show | 75% | 85% | 92%+ |
| Show → stay to offer | 80% | 88% | 95%+ |
| Offer-stay → buy (backend) | 8% | 18% | 30%+ |
| Workshop attendee → close | 5–12% | 12–22% | 22–40% |

Workshop close rates are 3–6× higher per attendee than free webinar because:
1. Attendee pre-committed financially (better qualification)
2. Attendee invested 3 hours (higher engagement)
3. Bonus: workshop-only pricing for backend
4. Better proof stacking (real teaching builds authority)

## Tech stack

- **Registration / payment:** ThriveCart / Stripe / SamCart
- **Hosting:** Zoom + StreamYard (for production quality) or pure Zoom
- **Worksheet delivery:** PDF or Notion / Google Doc
- **Recording + delivery:** Zoom Cloud or manual post-edit
- **Email:** standard stack

## Pricing logic

- **$47 workshop:** low-barrier. Filters casual listeners, not deep-pocket buyers. Good for lead-gen-to-mid-ticket ($2K–$5K).
- **$197 workshop:** filters harder. Skews toward serious buyers. Pairs with mid-ticket ($5K–$15K) backend.
- **$497 workshop:** premium. Attendees expect deep value. Pairs with high-ticket ($10K–$50K) backend.
- **$997 workshop:** exclusive / advanced. Small audience, very high close rate on backend.

## Common failure modes

1. **Workshop that's a pitch in disguise.** Attendees paid — they're paying for teaching, not a 2-hour infomercial. Fix: 80% teach, 20% pitch.
2. **Too much teach, no close.** Attendees leave happy without buying. Fix: build the offer into the teaching — show what the mechanism produces when fully implemented (the backend).
3. **Tonal whiplash at offer.** Sudden shift from teacher to salesperson. Fix: explicit acknowledgment — "I'm going to share one more thing — what happens next if you want to keep going."
4. **Cheap price reveals workshop as funnel.** $7 workshop signals "this is really an ad." Fix: $47 minimum; $197+ preferred.
5. **No Q&A or rushed Q&A.** Attendees feel unheard. Fix: 3 Q&A clusters minimum across the workshop.
6. **Recording delivered late.** Non-attendees + late-attendees lose the offer window. Fix: 48-hour turnaround max.
7. **No workshop-attendee-only pricing.** No reason to attend live vs replay. Fix: special pricing for live attendees that expires after workshop.
