# Multi-Video Pre-Launch Runway — Archetype Playbook

> **When it wins:** Cohort launches where the audience benefits from an education arc before the offer ask. 3–5 video runway teaches and primes the list, followed by a cart-open event. Generic version of the 3-video pre-launch pattern.

## When to pick this archetype

- Cohort-delivered offer ($997–$10K typical).
- Audience benefits from 7–21 days of education before the offer ask.
- Existing email list / social audience can consume the pre-launch content.
- You can produce polished pre-launch videos (15–30 min each).
- You have 2–4 launches per year, each with a 1–3 week runway.

## Phase 1 — Pre-Launch (weeks -10 to -1)

### Weeks -10 to -6: Strategy + video production
- Nail the transformation the runway will teach
- Map the 3–5 video structure:
  - Video 1: "The What / Why" — reframe the problem, introduce the category
  - Video 2: "The How" — teach the core mechanism
  - Video 3: "The What If / Ownership" — what's possible after implementing
  - (Optional Video 4): deeper mechanism teach OR behind-the-scenes
  - Video 4 / 5: cart-open event (offer reveal)
- Script each video (20–40 pages typical)
- Production: film, edit, polish. Each video ≥ 15 min.
- Asset consistency: visual identity across all videos

### Weeks -5 to -3: Runway landing + email automation
- Runway "vault" landing page (unlocks videos as they release)
- Pre-runway opt-in page: "Get early access to the training series"
- Email sequences:
  - Pre-runway warming (3–5 emails before runway starts)
  - Runway email cadence (one email per day with each video drop)
  - Cart-open sequence (5–10 emails once cart opens)
  - Cart-close sequence
- Community / DM engagement plan

### Weeks -2 to -1: Traffic + warming
- Paid-ad campaign to drive pre-runway opt-ins
- Partner / affiliate notifications
- Social teasers (daily posts week-of)
- Last-minute QA: email automation, video hosting, landing pages

## Phase 2 — Runway (week 0)

### Day 1: Runway opens
- Welcome email with Video 1 link
- Social posts pointing to Video 1
- Community thread for Video 1 discussion
- Engagement metrics tracked: Video 1 watch rate, completion rate, comments

### Days 2–6 (Video 2 + Video 3 drop)
- Day 3 (Video 2): email + social + community
- Day 5 (Video 3): email + social + community
- Engagement posts between videos: "Video 2 discussion thread," "what's your biggest takeaway from Video 1?"
- Live Q&A or community event between videos (optional, high-leverage)

### Metrics to track daily during runway
- Email open rate per drop
- Video watch rate + completion rate
- Community engagement (comments, replies, reactions)
- Un-subscribe / attrition rate

## Phase 3 — Momentum — Cart Opens (Day 7 or equivalent)

### Cart-open event (webinar OR polished offer video)
- 60–90 min
- Structure:
  - Recap of runway journey (5–10 min)
  - Bridge to offer: "Now that we've established [beliefs], here's the vehicle"
  - Offer introduction (core + bonuses + guarantee)
  - Stack slide + price reveal
  - Urgency: real cohort seat cap + cart-close timestamp
  - Live Q&A + close (15–30 min)

### Cart-open-day communication
- Pre-event: reminder email + SMS
- Post-event: replay email + first cart-open email
- Social posts throughout day
- DMs to top-engagement prospects

## Phase 4 — Follow-Up — Cart Window (3–7 days)

### Cart-window email sequence (5–10 emails)
- Day 1: cart-open replay + core offer
- Day 2: objection reframe (money / timing)
- Day 3: proof stories / testimonials
- Day 4: FAQ / value recap
- Day 5 (last day, or earlier): urgency sequence

### Last 24–48 hours
- Morning: "24 hours left"
- Mid-day: bonus expiration
- Evening: final-hours setup
- Last hour: simple close email + community push

### Close discipline
- Hard cart close at stated time. Protects future launch integrity.
- Post-close email: thanks + next-cohort wait-list

## Phase 5 — Analyze (weeks +1 to +3)

### Post-launch debrief
- Opt-in → runway-watch attrition at each video
- Runway-watch → cart-open attendance
- Cart-open attendance → purchase
- Revenue vs goal
- Top-performing runway video (which earned most closes)
- Top objections addressed in cart window

### Cohort onboarding (weeks +1 to +3)
- Welcome + orientation
- First live call (if applicable)
- Community activation
- First-win engineering

### Next-runway improvements
- Which video had highest drop-off — rewrite for next launch
- Which email converted best — extract template
- Which objection reframe landed best — catalog

## Runway structure variants

### 3-video runway (default, 7-day)
- V1: Why
- V2: How
- V3: What if + cart open
- Best for: most cohort launches, broad market fit

### 4-video runway (14-day)
- V1: Why
- V2: How (part 1)
- V3: How (part 2)
- V4: What if + cart open
- Best for: complex offers, longer sales cycles

### 5-video runway (21-day)
- V1: Foundational reframe
- V2: Mechanism part 1
- V3: Mechanism part 2
- V4: Ownership / vision
- V5: Cart-open event
- Best for: high-ticket ($5K+), high-consideration buyers

### Daily micro-runway (7-day, 7 short videos)
- 7 × 5–10 min videos, one per day
- Best for: impatient audiences, content-native creators

## Common failure modes

1. **Video 1 pitches.** Destroys runway credibility. Fix: V1 teaches, never sells. Pitching starts at cart-open event.
2. **Video production quality inconsistent.** Feels amateur. Fix: same director / producer / visual language across all videos.
3. **Gaps too long between videos.** Momentum dies at 5+ days. Fix: max 3–4 days between drops.
4. **No engagement mechanism.** One-way broadcast → low retention. Fix: community threads, live Q&A, DM engagement between videos.
5. **Cart-open event tonal whiplash.** Sudden infomercial pivot. Fix: frame cart-open as "the natural next step of what we just taught."
6. **Cart window too long (>7 days).** Close rate drops + attention drifts. Fix: 3–5 days typical; 7 max.
