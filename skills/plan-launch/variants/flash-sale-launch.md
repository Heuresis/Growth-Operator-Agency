# Flash Sale Launch — Archetype Playbook

> **When it wins:** Hot audience + real urgency + short window (3–7 days). Revenue burst from relationship capital. No new acquisition — this is monetization of existing trust.

## When to pick this archetype

- Engaged list (2K+ with 25%+ open rate) or community.
- Offer pre-exists and is proven (not testing a new offer).
- Real urgency anchor (bonus expiring, price increase, seat cap, holiday, anniversary, cohort-specific opening).
- Frequency budget: 3–6 flash sales per year max; more than that fatigues the list.
- You're ok with 30–60 days of post-sale "quiet" to protect list relationship.

## Phase 1 — Pre-Launch (1–2 weeks before)

### Week -2: Planning
- Define the real urgency mechanism — no fake countdowns
- Set the revenue goal (based on list size × historical conversion × current offer price)
- Choose the bonus / incentive that's time-limited
- Draft the flash-sale sequence (5–10 emails in 3–7 days)
- Prepare community / social posts
- Tech QA: checkout, payment processor, email automation, landing page

### Week -1: Warm-up
- Teaser emails (1–2) building anticipation: "Something's coming"
- Social teasers (Stories / LinkedIn posts / X)
- Internal team briefing: who handles inbound DMs, support, tech issues
- Partner / affiliate briefing if applicable
- Final asset QA

### Days -3 to 0: Soft-warm
- Day -3: pre-announcement post in community / email teaser
- Day -2: "opens in 48 hours" email
- Day -1: "opens tomorrow" email + social
- Day 0 morning: cart open

## Phase 2 — Launch Day (Day 1 of sale)

### Morning
- Cart-open email (8–10 AM typical)
- SMS notification to opt-in list
- Community pinned post + announcement
- Social posts across every active channel
- Personal DMs to 10–30 top-engagement prospects

### Throughout Day 1
- Monitor revenue vs target
- Share proof in real-time: "just had 12 people enroll in the first hour"
- Respond to DMs / replies within 1 hour
- End-of-day #1 email with Day 2 push

### Typical Day 1 metrics
- 30–50% of total flash-sale revenue usually comes in Day 1 (if sale is 3-day)
- 15–25% if sale is 5–7 day
- Early signal: if Day 1 is below 20% of goal, escalate community / SMS push

## Phase 3 — Momentum (Days 2 through N-1)

### Daily email rhythm
- **Day 2:** address the top objection (usually money or timing)
- **Day 3:** share a proof story / testimonial / case study
- **Day 4 (if 5-day):** bonus recap OR deeper value share
- **Day 5 (if 5-day):** FAQ email

### Community push daily
- 1–2 posts in community with new angles
- Featured buyer / early adopter spotlight
- Q&A thread or live session if possible
- Peer-to-peer visibility

### Mid-flash dip
- Close rate naturally dips mid-sale. Don't panic. Plan for Day 2–3 being quieter.
- Rescue moves: bonus reveal, time-sensitive live Q&A, community-specific perk

## Phase 4 — Follow-Up (last 24–48 hours)

### 24 hours before close
- Morning: "24 hours left" email
- Mid-day: bonus expiration reminder
- Evening: final-hours setup email

### Final 12 hours
- 12-hour email: last chance
- 6-hour email: specific proof + FAQ
- 2-hour email: simple CTA
- Final hour: community push + personal DMs to fence-sitters

### The close
- Cart closes EXACTLY when stated. No extensions. Extensions train the list to wait.
- Post-close email: "Thanks for watching; next opportunity will be [when]"
- Non-closers → wait-list or nurture continues

## Phase 5 — Analyze (within 1 week)

### Flash-sale debrief (`/launch-report` compressed)
- Revenue vs goal
- Revenue breakdown by day
- Conversion rate by day / email / channel
- Open / click / conversion per email
- Community engagement during window
- Refund / chargeback early indicators

### Key learnings
- Which email drove most sales
- Which day of the window was strongest
- Which objection reframe landed / flopped
- Which bonus was most referenced in DMs / replies

### Protect the list (weeks +1 to +4)
- Go quiet on promotional content for 30–60 days
- Deliver pure value broadcasts (no offers) during the quiet period
- Re-emerge when list relationship has recovered

## Metrics dashboard

| Metric | Target | Red flag |
|---|---|---|
| Day 1 revenue | 25–40% of goal | <15% |
| Total sale revenue | Hit or exceed goal | Miss by >30% |
| Blended list → revenue | 1–5% | <0.5% |
| Refund rate 30 days post | <8% | >12% |
| Next-broadcast open rate | Within 10% of baseline | >15% drop (list fatigue) |

## Common failure modes

1. **Fake urgency.** "Only 5 seats!" when there are 500. Trust dies. Fix: real mechanisms only.
2. **Too frequent flash sales.** List fatigues → open rates drop → revenue drops. Fix: 4 per year max.
3. **Discount-addicted list.** List waits for next sale. Fix: flash-sell bonuses / bundles / seats, not the core-offer price.
4. **No teaser warm-up.** Cold open = low Day 1. Fix: 48-hour pre-open teaser.
5. **Extending the cart.** Signals future extensions will come. Fix: hold the deadline.
6. **No post-sale silence.** Immediate next promo → list exhausted. Fix: 30–60 day quiet period of pure-value broadcasts.
7. **No community activation.** Email-only → weak peer-driven reach. Fix: daily community touches during sale.
