# JV / Partner-Stack Launch — Archetype Playbook

> **When it wins:** Partner audience access + coordinated promotion window. 3–10+ partners stack their lists to drive a single offer's cart window. Common for $997–$10K cohort launches where list ceiling is the constraint.

## When to pick this archetype

- Your list alone can't hit the revenue target.
- You have (or can build) 3–10+ partner relationships in the same ICP.
- Partners have non-competing but complementary offers.
- You can offer meaningful affiliate commissions (30–50% typical).
- Launch cadence: 1–2 per year typically (JV partners don't promote you every month).

## Phase 1 — Pre-Launch (weeks -12 to -1)

### Weeks -12 to -8: Partner recruitment
- Identify 20–40 potential partners in ICP
- Vet for list size + engagement + alignment
- Draft partner pitch: "What's in it for them + my list + timeline"
- Personal outreach to 20 (expect 3–10 confirms)
- Affiliate agreement drafted (`reference/legal-templates/affiliate-agreement.md`)

### Weeks -8 to -4: Partner onboarding
- Partner agreements signed
- Swipe copy delivered (email templates, social posts, ad creative)
- Partner tracking URLs generated
- Affiliate dashboard access
- Partner briefing call(s) with expectations + timeline + materials

### Weeks -4 to -2: Asset production
- Standard launch assets (sales page, webinar, emails, etc.)
- Partner-specific materials (co-branded sales page variants optional, custom bonuses for top partners optional)
- Launch calendar shared with partners (who promotes when)

### Weeks -2 to -1: Partner activation
- Partner teaser emails begin
- Partner social posts begin
- Creator's own list teasers
- Paid ads preparing to scale partner-referred traffic
- Partner leaderboard / incentive announced (contest for top partner)

## Phase 2 — Launch Day (cart opens)

### The morning
- Creator's own cart-open email
- Partner cart-open emails (staggered or simultaneous per plan)
- Partner social posts + community pushes
- Creator's social / community push
- Ads scale to capture partner-referred traffic + retargeting

### Partner coordination
- Slack / Discord channel for real-time partner updates
- Shared leaderboard updates (who's driving what)
- Troubleshoot tracking / attribution issues same-day
- Recognition posts (shout-outs) for early-driving partners

### Metrics to track on Day 1
- Revenue by partner
- Revenue by creator's own list
- Tracking link clicks per partner
- Affiliate cookie / attribution verified

## Phase 3 — Momentum (days 2 to N-1)

### Daily partner touches
- Daily email / Slack update to partners with leaderboard + push points
- Featured-partner spotlights (reward performance with visibility)
- Mid-window re-activation for partners who haven't promoted yet
- Co-marketing opportunities (partner interviews, guest posts in community)

### Creator's own promotion continues
- Daily email to own list
- Social posts
- Community engagement
- Retargeting ads

### Mid-window interventions
- Partners who promised but haven't promoted → personal nudge
- Top-driving partners → thank-you call or surprise bonus
- Tracking issues → resolve within 24 hours

## Phase 4 — Follow-Up (last 24–48 hours)

### 24 hours before close
- Creator's "24 hours" email
- Partner "24 hours" prompt with swipe copy
- Bonus expiration reminders
- Final leaderboard push (contest urgency)

### Final 12 hours
- Standard final-hours sequence on creator's list
- Partner final-hours swipe (optional)
- Last-minute partner asks via Slack / DM

### Cart close
- Hard close at stated time
- Post-close email to creator's list
- Partner debrief message

## Phase 5 — Analyze + Settle (weeks +1 to +4)

### Partner settlement
- Affiliate commissions calculated
- Refund-clawback schedule accounted for
- Payment processed within 45 days typically (per affiliate agreement)
- Top-partner bonus / contest prize delivered

### Debrief
- Total revenue + revenue-by-source breakdown
- Partner performance: who drove, who didn't, who will be invited next time
- Attribution issues documented
- Cohort onboarding standard

### Partner relationship continuation
- Thank-you note or gift to all promoting partners
- Case-study offer for top partners (you promote their launch next)
- List-swap or co-webinar opportunity
- Long-term relationship cultivation

## Communication calendar (simplified, 7-day cart window)

| Phase | Creator's list | Partner lists | Partner coordination |
|---|---|---|---|
| Pre-launch (-4 weeks) | Teaser (2 emails) | Partner internal briefings | Weekly partner update |
| Week -1 | Warmup (3 emails) | Teaser starts (1–2 emails) | Daily Slack + swipe drops |
| Day 0 (open) | Cart open (1 email) | Partner cart-open (1 email each) | Real-time Slack coord |
| Days 1–5 | Daily (5 emails) | 1–3 emails per partner | Daily leaderboard |
| Days 6–7 (close) | Final hours (3 emails) | Partners push final (1–2 emails) | Final-hours urgency |

## Asset checklist

- [ ] Affiliate agreements signed
- [ ] Affiliate tracking URLs + dashboard
- [ ] Swipe copy (email templates × 5, social posts × 10, ad creative × 5)
- [ ] Partner onboarding deck / video
- [ ] Partner Slack / Discord channel
- [ ] Leaderboard / tracking dashboard
- [ ] Partner contest / incentive
- [ ] Standard launch assets (sales page, webinar, emails, etc.)
- [ ] Refund + clawback schedule documented

## Partner commission structures

- **Standard:** 30–50% of first payment (one-time commissions)
- **Tiered:** 30% standard, 40% if partner drives 5+ sales, 50% if 10+ sales
- **Lifetime:** 30% of first + 20% of continuity for 12 months (higher total, more complex tracking)
- **Top-partner bonus:** contest prize for #1 partner (cash, gift, event ticket)

Higher commissions make partner recruiting easier; lower commissions preserve margin. $2K offer × 40% commission = $800 per sale to partner. Must fit your LTV:CAC math.

## Metrics dashboard

| Metric | Target | Red flag |
|---|---|---|
| Partners recruited | 5–10 | <3 |
| Partners who actually promote | 70%+ of recruited | <50% |
| Revenue from partners | 30–60% of total | <20% |
| Creator's own list contribution | 30–50% of total | <20% |
| Affiliate refund-clawback rate | <8% | >15% |

## Common failure modes

1. **Partners agree but don't promote.** Promise-to-action gap of 30–50%. Fix: tighter vetting + better swipe + incentive + personal nudges.
2. **Attribution disputes.** Partner claims sale that the creator's email drove. Fix: last-click attribution defined in agreement, single source of truth.
3. **Over-commission damages margin.** 60%+ commissions on a low-LTV offer breaks. Fix: model LTV:CAC with affiliate commission as CAC before signing.
4. **Partner list fatigue.** Partners promote same week → list fatigue. Fix: coordinate partner timing so lists don't get 10 emails at once.
5. **Low-quality partner traffic.** Partner list doesn't match ICP → low close rate + high refund. Fix: better partner-fit vetting.
6. **Affiliate-attracted "deal hunters" inflate refunds.** 30-day refund rate spikes. Fix: conditional refund policy + clawback schedule that protects against this pattern.
7. **No partner nurture post-launch.** Partners go cold. Fix: thank-you, case-study exchange, next-launch invite.
