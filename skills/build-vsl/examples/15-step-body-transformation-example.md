# the VSL director 15-Step VSL - Elite Body Transformation for Tech Founders

> **Framework:** Eli the VSL director's 15-Step VSL (Variant A) applied to the Fitness Coaching offer
> **Runtime:** 32-38 min (high-ticket, high-sophistication audience)
> **Target conversion:** 10%+ (the VSL director's invariant)
> **Price point:** $3,997/quarter core tier (FFOS Group Coaching)

## Context

This example walks the 15-Step VSL sequence through the canonical Fitness Coaching offer, calibrated for a high-sophistication audience. Because the audience is sophisticated and the price is $3,997+, the Hero Story runs 2-4 minutes (not the 5-8 min low-sophistication variant), Price Anchoring uses specific cost-of-inaction math, and Future Pace stays grounded and data-driven rather than lifestyle-fantasy. Copy invariant honored: no selling before Step 9.

## The Encoded VSL (15 Steps)

### Step 1 - Hook (0-30 sec) [Belief #1]

"If you're a tech founder operating at 5 out of 10, I'll show you the 90-day protocol I used to lose 18 pounds, fix my HRV, and get my decision quality back while raising a Series A. Not another fitness program. An operating system for your body."

### Step 2 - Lead (30 sec-2 min) [Belief #2]

"Over 28 minutes, I'll show you why every fitness program you've tried has failed by Week 3. Not discipline. The program was built for someone with a predictable schedule, a kitchen, and a boss they can't be late for. You have none of those. This is a system that treats fitness the same way you'd treat any startup problem."

### Step 3 - Qualification (2-4 min) [Belief #1 reinforced]

"For founders at $500K-$10M+ ARR who've gained 15-30 pounds. Not pre-revenue founders. Not for 30-day beach body or bodybuilding. Not for founders who want a meal plan they'll ignore."

### Step 4 - Problem & Question (4-7 min) [Belief #4 start]

"You think the problem is time. It's not. Every fitness system assumes a stable life, and you don't live one. The industry's answer: 'be more disciplined.' That fails because discipline is a willpower budget you already spent on company decisions. By Friday your tank is at zero. This is an operations problem, and you are being sold motivation."

### Step 5 - Hero Story + Guru Intro (7-10 min) [Belief #7]

**2-4 min calibration, high-sophistication audience.**

*Setup:* "Three years ago I was running a Series A SaaS company. 18 months in, 207 pounds, 22 heavier than when I started."

*Escalation:* "Noom for six weeks. An in-person trainer at $300/session, canceled eight times last month. A keto-coach on Twitter, $1,500 program, quit Week 4 during a launch."

*Dark Night:* "March 14, 9:42 PM, hotel bathroom in San Francisco. Shirt didn't button. Accepting a design award next morning. Texted my wife: 'I don't know what to do.'"

*Gift:* "My CTO: 'You solve complex systems problems every day. Why are you approaching your body differently?' I stopped treating fitness as willpower. 90 days later: 18 pounds down, HRV up 22%, sleep at 7+ hours."

### Step 6 - Solution Introduction (10-14 min) [Beliefs #3, #5]

"It's called the Founder Fitness Operating System, FFOS. Three layers. Founder-Calibrated Training: 30-minute sessions with gym, hotel, and home variants. Executive Nutrition Protocol: high-protein frameworks for restaurants, delivery, travel, zero meal prep. Recovery Intelligence: WHOOP/Oura data integrated into coaching decisions. You don't need more discipline. You need delegation, metrics, and infrastructure."

### Step 7 - Features & Benefits (14-18 min) [Beliefs #3, #6]

- Founder-Mode Workouts → "Program survives your worst week, not just your best."
- Restaurant Playbook (25+ chains) → "Your fitness never fights your company."
- Weekly Wearable Review → "Never overtrain during high-stress weeks."

### Step 8 - Testimonials & Receipts (18-22 min) [Beliefs #3, #7, #8]

Isomorphic testimonials matched to the prospect. Video of a founder who lost 22 lbs during a fundraising year. WHOOP HRV trend. Case study: 3am to 10pm sleep over 60 days with ARR chart showing company growth in the same window.

### Step 9 - Price Anchoring (22-26 min) [Beliefs #5, #6]

"How much is it worth for your decision quality at 9 of 10 vs 5 of 10? $5M ARR and 40% below peak: 5% growth-trajectory hit is $250K/year. Compare to Equinox Optimize at $40K/year, the ultra-premium science-based founder-fitness program at $20K+, or assembled separately at $11,900-$18,500/quarter. **FFOS Group Coaching is $3,997/quarter.**"

### Step 10 - Guarantee (26-28 min) [Beliefs #3, #7]

"Complete the 90-day protocol. 80% of coaching calls, 5 days of training a week, weekly check-ins. If you haven't lost at least 10 pounds and measurably improved your energy, we coach you another 90 days free until you do."

### Step 11 - Urgency Story (28-30 min) [Belief #4]

"We cap each cohort at 20 founders. That's not marketing. A 60-minute call with 20 people allows 3 minutes per person. Above 20 it becomes a lecture. 4 cohorts per year. If this one is full, you're on the waitlist for 90 days."

### Step 12 - Crossroads Close (30-33 min)

"Path 1: You do nothing. 12 months from now you'll be 5-10 pounds heavier, HRV lower, another $8-15K spent on generic programs. Still operating at 5 of 10. Path 2: You apply today. 90 days from now you're 15-20 pounds lighter, sleep at 7+, HRV trending up, walking into every board meeting at a different level. Which version of yourself do you want to be a year from now?"

### Step 13 - Takeaway Close (33-35 min)

"This isn't for everyone. Not willing to train 5 days at 30 minutes? Don't apply. Not willing to attend 80% of calls? Don't apply. I'd rather work with 20 committed founders than 40 tire-kickers. If you've watched this far, you're probably not a tire-kicker."

### Step 14 - Help Them Buy (35-37 min)

"Click the Apply Now button below. 7-minute application. If you qualify, a 30-minute call. Once you join, login credentials within 60 seconds and your coach reaches out within 24 hours."

### Step 15 - Future Pace (37-38 min) [Beliefs #1, #2]

"Once you join: Week 1 sleep improves. Week 2 afternoon energy returns. Week 6 shirts fit differently. Day 90 you read the 10-page performance report, body metrics alongside business metrics. You become the founder others ask: 'how do you stay in this shape while running a company?'"

## Why This Is A Good Example

- **Length calibration correct for audience sophistication.** Hero Story 2-4 min (not 5-8) because tech founders are high-sophistication. Future Pace is data-driven (10-page report, KPIs) rather than lifestyle fantasy.
- **Mechanism threads 5 places.** "Founder Fitness Operating System" in Hook (implicit), Problem (contrast), Solution (named), Testimonials, Crossroads. the VSL director invariant honored.
- **Never sells before Step 9.** First price at minute 22+, after 8 beliefs installed. Price Anchoring uses self-generated math, not value stacks.
