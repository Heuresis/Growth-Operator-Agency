# Weekly Review Template (60 min)

## Purpose
60-minute weekly rhythm to review leading + lagging metrics, validate whether the right work is happening, and plan the next week. One per department + one rollup.

## Cadence
- **When**: Friday afternoon OR Monday morning (pick one and hold it)
- **Duration**: 60 min, hard stop
- **Format**: video call with shared doc, or async write-up + 15-min discussion
- **Owner**: department heads; rollup owned by founder or ops director

## Agenda
1. **Metrics Review** (15 min) — leading + lagging side-by-side
2. **Wins** (5 min) — top 3 wins + what produced them
3. **Misses** (10 min) — top 3 misses + root cause + owner
4. **Decisions** (10 min) — what we are changing next week
5. **Next-Week Plan** (15 min) — top 3 priorities per person
6. **Open Items** (5 min) — stuck blockers, unresolved risks

## Anti-patterns (block these)
- Metrics-only review with no decisions — diagnosis without action is theater
- Wins-only review — without misses you don't improve
- No owner on correction — action without name never happens
- Missing the review entirely — 2 skips triggers cadence reset conversation
