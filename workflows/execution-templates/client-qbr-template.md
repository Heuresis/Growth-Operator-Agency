# Client QBR Template (Quarterly Business Review)

## Purpose
90-minute quarterly business review with client. Consolidates the last 90 days into a single honest conversation: outcomes, spend, lessons, renewal, expansion. Produces the artifact that informs the renew/pause/offboard decision.

## Cadence
- **Frequency**: every 90 days per active engagement
- **Duration**: 90 min (60 min presentation + 30 min discussion)
- **Format**: video call with screen-share; written QBR doc delivered 24h before
- **Attendees (client)**: primary decision-maker required
- **Attendees (us)**: delivery lead + department heads; founder for key accounts

## QBR Document Structure

### Page 1 — Executive Summary
One page. The client should be able to read this alone and know whether to renew.
- Original goal (as scoped): `{goal statement}`
- 90-day outcome: `{quantified result}`
- On-track / ahead / behind + honest assessment
- Renewal recommendation: `{continue / expand / pause / off-board}`

### Page 2 — Goals vs Actuals
| Metric | Day-0 Baseline | 90-Day Target | 90-Day Actual | Delta |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| `{metric 1}` | `{n}` | `{n}` | `{n}` | `{%}` |
| `{metric 2}` | `{n}` | `{n}` | `{n}` | `{%}` |
| `{metric 3}` | `{n}` | `{n}` | `{n}` | `{%}` |

### Page 3 — What Was Shipped
Asset inventory. Every artifact the engagement produced.
- Foundations: `{icp.md, positioning.md, voice-guide.md, offer.md}`
- Sales: `{vsl-script.md, funnel-map.md}`
- Marketing: `{n posts, n pieces, n ads, top-3 list}`
- Nurture: `{n sequences, n emails live, avg open rate}`
- Launch: `{launch reports shipped}`
- Scale: `{SOPs, case studies, revenue reports}`
- Partnerships: `{partners onboarded}`

### Page 4 — What We Learned (Both Sides)
Our side:
- `{learning}` — action we are taking: `{change}`

Client side (fill collaboratively):
- `{learning}` — action they are taking: `{change}`

### Page 5 — Friction + Gaps
Honest list of where things didn't go smoothly.
- `{friction}` — root cause: `{cause}` — prevention: `{action}`

### Page 6 — Financial Summary
- Engagement investment: `{$}`
- Revenue attributed: `{$}` (period-matched)
- Attributable new pipeline: `{$}`
- ROAS / ROI: `{ratio}`
- Implied LTV uplift: `{$}` (if measurable)

### Page 7 — Next 90 Days
If renewing: what's the next 90-day plan?
- Top 3 goals
- Scope + deliverables
- Cadence
- Investment
- New risks / assumptions

If off-boarding: clean handoff plan.
- Asset inventory transferred
- Final documentation
- Post-engagement check-in date

## Live QBR Agenda (90 min)
1. **Opening** (3 min) — thank-you, state the purpose of the hour
2. **Executive Summary** (7 min) — walk page 1 out loud
3. **Goals vs Actuals** (15 min) — metric-by-metric, highlight ahead/behind
4. **What Shipped** (10 min) — asset inventory tour
5. **What We Learned** (15 min) — both sides share
6. **Friction + Gaps** (10 min) — honest conversation
7. **Financial Summary** (5 min) — ROI framing
8. **Next 90 Days** (20 min) — co-design if renewing; handoff if off-boarding
9. **Close** (5 min) — confirm next step + date

## Anti-patterns (block these)
- QBR without the decision-maker — reschedule
- QBR delivered live-only without written doc — doc is the record
- Recommendation buried — put on page 1
- Renewal conversation dodged — address explicitly even if answer is "pause"
- No next steps with owner + date — meeting was theater
