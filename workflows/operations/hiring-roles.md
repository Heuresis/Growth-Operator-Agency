# Hiring Roles Catalog

## Purpose
A 15-role catalog for the Growth Operator. Each role has an ideal profile, a scorecard, a comp band, and a 30/60/90 plan. Use this when writing a job spec, running an interview, or setting a new hire up for success. Comp bands are US 2026 baseline; adjust ±20% for region. All bands assume full-time unless noted.

## Hiring Sequence by Revenue Stage

| Stage | Revenue | First Hires (in order) |
|---|---|---|
| Build | $0-$500k | 1 VA, 1 Setter |
| Systemize | $500k-$1M | 1 Closer, 1 Content Mgr |
| Delegate | $1M-$3M | Ops Mgr, CS Mgr, Ad Buyer, Video Editor |
| Scale | $3M-$5M | Marketing Mgr, Part-Time CFO, 2nd Closer, Copywriter |
| Refactor | $5M-$10M | YouTube Producer, Coach, SDR team, Developer, 2nd Ops Mgr |

See `mode-of-operations.md` for the full hiring gate logic.

## 1. Setter (Appointment Setter / DM Setter)

**Ideal profile:** 1-3 years inside sales or high-volume outbound. Fast typist, empathetic writer, comfortable on 100+ DMs/day. Hungry — this is a ramp role.
**Reports to:** Closer or Ops Mgr
**Scorecard (weekly):**
- DMs sent: 300+ / week
- Reply rate: >25%
- Qualified calls booked: 10+ / week
- Show rate on booked calls: >65%
**Comp:** $3-5k base/mo + $100-$200 per showed call + $500 closed-deal bonus. OTE $60-90k.
**30/60/90:**
- 30: shadow senior setter, send 50 DMs/day under review, learn ICP + objection library
- 60: full volume, hitting 70% of target reply rate
- 90: at or above target; can handle 1 new objection type unassisted

## 2. Closer (Account Executive)

**Ideal profile:** 3-5 years closing high-ticket ($3k-$30k) offers, consultative sell, strong discovery. Prior vertical experience preferred.
**Reports to:** Operator (at <$3M), Sales Mgr (at $3M+)
**Scorecard (monthly):**
- Calls taken: 40-80/mo
- Close rate: >25%
- ACV: at or above target
- Pipeline in CRM: updated within 24h of every call
- Collection rate: >95%
**Comp:** $4-8k base/mo + 8-12% commission on collected revenue. OTE $120-250k.
**30/60/90:**
- 30: shadow 10 Operator-led calls, pass objection quiz, take 5 calls with review
- 60: full call volume, close rate within 70% of team avg
- 90: at or above team avg close rate; owns 1 objection training for setters

## 3. SDR (Sales Development Rep — Outbound)

**Ideal profile:** 0-2 years. Different from Setter: SDRs run cold outbound (email, LinkedIn, phone) to net-new accounts. Setters work inbound leads.
**Reports to:** Marketing Mgr or Sales Mgr
**Scorecard (weekly):**
- Touches: 400+/wk (email + LinkedIn + calls)
- Meetings booked: 5-10/wk
- Meeting → opp conversion: >40%
- CRM hygiene: 100% of touches logged
**Comp:** $3.5-5.5k base/mo + $150-300 per meeting held + kicker on closed. OTE $65-95k.
**30/60/90:** 30: ICP training + cadence training; 60: 70% of touch target; 90: full volume + 1 meeting held/day.

## 4. Content Manager

**Ideal profile:** 2-4 years running content across 2+ formats (written + video, or social + long-form). Editorial calendar discipline, comfortable briefing creators.
**Reports to:** Marketing Mgr or Operator
**Scorecard (weekly):**
- Pieces published: per content plan (e.g., 3 long-form + 5 short-form)
- On-time publish rate: >90%
- Content → pipeline attribution: tracked
- Net new subscribers/followers: target-based
**Comp:** $5-8k base/mo, optional 0.5-1% of attributed revenue. OTE $70-110k.
**30/60/90:** 30: audit existing content + draft content plan; 60: plan operationalized, first 4 weeks shipped on time; 90: full cycle running, 1 repeatable series launched.

## 5. Video Editor

**Ideal profile:** 2+ years editing short-form (Reels/Shorts/TikTok) + long-form (YouTube). Fluent in Premiere or DaVinci Resolve. Can turn around a 60-second clip in <2 hours.
**Reports to:** Content Mgr or YouTube Producer
**Scorecard (weekly):**
- Cuts delivered: per plan (e.g., 15 short-form + 1 long-form)
- Turnaround time: <48h from raw footage
- Revision rate: <1.5 rounds per cut
**Comp:** $3-6k base/mo (FT) or $40-80/hr (contract). Offshore $1.5-3.5k/mo.
**30/60/90:** 30: brand style guide absorbed, 5 cuts approved first round; 60: full volume; 90: owns 1 format end-to-end (thumbnails, packaging, upload).

## 6. CS Manager (Customer Success / Delivery)

**Ideal profile:** 3-5 years in CS, coaching, or delivery for a high-touch service. Deeply empathetic, comfortable running group calls + 1:1s, builds retention systems.
**Reports to:** Operator or Ops Mgr
**Scorecard (monthly):**
- NPS: >50
- Churn: <5%/mo
- Client milestone hit rate: >75%
- Upsell / referral rate: >10% of active clients/quarter
**Comp:** $5-9k base/mo + retention bonus ($100-500/client retained past 6mo). OTE $80-140k.
**30/60/90:** 30: onboard shadow 10 clients, map current CS workflow; 60: lead own client cohort; 90: propose + ship 1 retention improvement with measurable lift.

## 7. VA (Virtual Assistant / Executive Assistant)

**Ideal profile:** 1-3 years supporting a founder. Calendar wizard, inbox-zero capable, proactive not reactive. Often offshore.
**Reports to:** Operator directly
**Scorecard (weekly):**
- Inbox kept <10 unread during working hours
- Calendar 100% accurate, conflict-free
- Tasks completed on time: >90%
- Weekly priorities document sent every Sunday 6pm local
**Comp:** $1.5-3.5k/mo (offshore), $4-6k/mo (onshore).
**30/60/90:** 30: absorb Operator's calendar + inbox rules; 60: unblocked for 80% of routine tasks; 90: owns 3 named recurring processes end-to-end.

## 8. Marketing Manager

**Ideal profile:** 4-7 years running acquisition for a $1M-$10M brand. Fluent across paid, organic, content, email. Manages 2-5 contractors/staff.
**Reports to:** Operator
**Scorecard (monthly):**
- CAC within target
- MQL → SQL conversion: >30%
- Leads delivered: per plan
- Content cadence: 100% on plan
- Attribution reporting on time, monthly
**Comp:** $8-14k base/mo + 0.5-2% of attributed revenue. OTE $140-220k.
**30/60/90:** 30: full audit + 90-day plan; 60: plan shipping + first report; 90: first measurable lift on CAC or lead volume.

## 9. Ops Manager (Operations Manager / COO-lite)

**Ideal profile:** 5-8 years running ops for a service or SaaS business. Hires/fires, builds SOPs, owns the number. Eventually becomes Chief of Staff or COO.
**Reports to:** Operator
**Scorecard (monthly):**
- SOP library growth: 2+ new or updated/mo
- Direct reports' scorecard avg: >3.0
- Operational KPI dashboard: on time, accurate
- Hiring pipeline: always 2+ candidates in funnel for open roles
**Comp:** $10-16k base/mo + 1-2% of revenue growth above plan. OTE $150-250k.
**30/60/90:** 30: audit ops stack + SOPs + team performance; 60: first 3 high-ROI changes live; 90: owns weekly leadership cadence + 1 hire closed.

## 10. Part-Time CFO (Fractional Finance)

**Ideal profile:** CPA or equivalent, fractional CFO for 3+ other SMBs, fluent in services and SaaS economics. 5-10 hrs/week.
**Reports to:** Operator
**Scorecard (monthly):**
- Close books by day 10 of following month
- 13-week cash flow accurate within 5%
- Monthly P&L + commentary delivered
- 1 named cost-reduction or revenue-driver initiative/quarter
**Comp:** $2-5k/mo retainer for 5-10 hrs/wk, or $200-400/hr.
**30/60/90:** 30: financial system audit, QuickBooks/Xero cleanup; 60: first clean monthly close; 90: 13-week cash model + 3-year plan.

## 11. Coach (Program Coach / Delivery Coach)

**Ideal profile:** Practitioner who has personally achieved the outcome the program promises. 2-5 years coaching group + 1:1 settings.
**Reports to:** CS Mgr
**Scorecard (monthly):**
- Coaching call attendance: >70% of enrolled clients
- Client milestone hit rate: >75%
- NPS of coaching experience: >60
- Testimonials/case studies sourced: 1+/mo
**Comp:** $5-10k base/mo + $100-500 per client milestone hit. OTE $80-160k.
**30/60/90:** 30: shadow + take first cohort under review; 60: solo cohort; 90: own a repeatable module end-to-end.

## 12. YouTube Producer

**Ideal profile:** 3+ years producing YouTube channels >100k subs. Owns idea-to-publish: research, scripting, directing recordings, thumbnail/title optimization, publish, analytics review.
**Reports to:** Content Mgr or Marketing Mgr
**Scorecard (monthly):**
- Videos published: per plan (typically 2-4/mo)
- Avg CTR: >5%
- Avg view duration: >40%
- Subscribers added: per plan
- Long-form → email/pipeline conversion: tracked
**Comp:** $6-10k base/mo + performance bonus tied to views or subs. OTE $90-160k.
**30/60/90:** 30: channel audit + 90-day slate; 60: first 4 videos shipped, packaging system live; 90: measurable CTR or watch-time lift vs baseline.

## 13. Ad Buyer (Paid Media Specialist)

**Ideal profile:** 3-5 years running paid on Meta, Google, YouTube. Has spent $1M+ across prior roles. Reads LTV/CAC, not just ROAS.
**Reports to:** Marketing Mgr
**Scorecard (weekly):**
- Cost per lead within target
- CAC within target
- Spend pacing ±10% of plan
- 2+ creative tests live at all times
- Weekly paid report on time
**Comp:** $6-10k base/mo + 5-10% of attributable profit lift. OTE $110-180k. Or agency retainer $3-8k/mo.
**30/60/90:** 30: account audit + first creative tests live; 60: CAC within 20% of target; 90: at or below CAC target, scalable account structure.

## 14. Copywriter

**Ideal profile:** 3-5 years writing direct-response for offers in similar category. Portfolio of sales pages, email sequences, VSLs, ad copy with measurable outcomes.
**Reports to:** Marketing Mgr or Content Mgr
**Scorecard (monthly):**
- Deliverables shipped: per plan
- Revision rate: <2 rounds per piece
- Conversion lift vs control: measured quarterly
- Turnaround: <5 business days standard
**Comp:** $5-9k/mo FT, or $2-8k per project. OTE $80-150k.
**30/60/90:** 30: voice + offer absorption, first 3 deliverables; 60: full volume, 1 A/B test live; 90: 1 measurable conversion lift.

## 15. Developer (Full-Stack / Automation)

**Ideal profile:** 4+ years full-stack (or 2+ yrs no-code stack if scope is internal tooling). Owns internal tools, integrations, automations. Not a product engineer — a growth engineer.
**Reports to:** Ops Mgr or Operator
**Scorecard (monthly):**
- Tickets shipped: per sprint plan
- Uptime on owned systems: >99%
- Automation hours saved per month: tracked
- 1 measurable operational improvement/quarter
**Comp:** $8-14k base/mo FT, or $80-150/hr contract.
**30/60/90:** 30: system map + tool audit; 60: first 3 high-ROI automations live; 90: 20+ hrs/week of team time saved, documented.

```
_______

  1. _______
  2. _______
  3. _______

Competencies (ranked 1-5 for candidate eval):
  1. _______
  2. _______
  3. _______
  4. _______
  5. _______

Comp band: base $____ / variable $____ / OTE $____
Start date: _______
30/60/90 plan: linked to Notion
```

## Interview Loop (Standard)

1. Screen (30 min, recruiter or hiring mgr) — culture + basics
2. Scorecard interview (60 min, hiring mgr) — past outcomes tied to scorecard lines
3. Case study / work sample (take-home, 2-4 hrs of candidate time, paid if >2h)
4. Panel (2x 45 min with future peers)
5. Reference calls (2 minimum, ideally former direct mgr)
6. Operator final (30 min) — values + mutual selection
7. Offer within 48h of final round

See `skills/hiring-brief/` for the briefing flow and `skills/reference-check/` for the call script.

## Metrics / Success Criteria
- Time-to-fill: <45 days from spec approved to offer signed
- Offer acceptance rate: >75%
- 90-day retention: >90%
- Quota/ramp hit rate at day 90: >70%
- First-year regrettable attrition: <10%

## Failure Modes
- **Hiring without a scorecard.** Fix: the scorecard template above is a gate — no posting without it.
- **Hiring for resume, not outcomes.** Fix: every interview line must map to a scorecard outcome.
- **Comp band guesswork.** Fix: refresh bands annually against Pave/Levels/industry survey.
- **Onboarding by osmosis.** Fix: 30/60/90 plan assigned on day 0, reviewed at each gate.
- **Hiring too fast for revenue.** Fix: see `mode-of-operations.md` — stage-gate each hire against revenue triggers.
