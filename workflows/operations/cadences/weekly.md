# Weekly Cadences

## Purpose
The weekly rhythm that turns daily activity into weekly decisions and weekly outcomes into monthly direction. Six standing meetings; none optional. Total operator load: ~4 hours/week across all weekly cadences.

## Weekly Calendar

| Day | Time (PT) | Cadence | Duration | Driver |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Mon | 9:00am | Leadership weekly | 60 min | Operator |
| Mon | 10:30am | Sales weekly | 45 min | Sales lead / Closer |
| Tue | 10:00am | Marketing weekly | 45 min | Marketing Mgr |
| Wed | 11:00am | CS weekly | 45 min | CS Mgr |
| Thu | 10:00am | Content weekly | 45 min | Content Mgr |
| Fri | 3:00pm | All-hands | 30 min | Operator |

Fridays stay afternoon — keeps the team ending the week together. Mondays stay front-loaded — sets the week's priorities before anyone buries themselves in work.

---

## 1. Leadership Weekly (Mon 9:00am, 60 min)

**Participants:** Operator + all direct reports (Ops Mgr, Marketing Mgr, CS Mgr, Content Mgr, Sales lead, CFO if applicable)
**Agenda:**
- 0:00-0:10 — Numbers review: revenue, cash, leads, pipeline, CAC, churn (live dashboard)
- 0:10-0:25 — Department updates: each lead, 2 min each — what shipped, what ships this week, blocks
- 0:25-0:45 — Top 3 decisions this week (pre-circulated, RACI assigned)
- 0:45-0:55 — Operator priorities + asks of the room
- 0:55-1:00 — Actions + owners + due dates

**Pre-read (sent by Sunday 6pm):**
- Dashboard snapshot link
- Decision docs for the 3 items to decide
- Department leads' 2-bullet updates posted in #leadership channel

**Outputs:**
- Meeting recap in #leadership within 2 hours (template in `team-collaboration.md`)
- ClickUp tasks created for every action
- Decisions logged in Notion decision log

**Decisions made here:**
- Hiring go/no-go for open roles
- Tool purchases >$200/mo
- Campaign launches >$5k budget
- Customer escalations >$5k refund
- Comp changes >10%

## 2. Sales Weekly (Mon 10:30am, 45 min)

**Participants:** Sales lead (Closer or Sales Mgr) + all Closers + all Setters + Marketing Mgr (first 15 min only)
**Agenda:**
- 0:00-0:05 — Week in review: revenue booked, collected, show rate, close rate
- 0:05-0:15 — Pipeline walk: top 10 open opps by stage, committed closes this week (with Marketing Mgr present)
- 0:15-0:25 — Funnel diagnostics: where did we leak last week? (at-stage conversion)
- 0:25-0:35 — Script / offer changes: what's working, what's testing this week
- 0:35-0:42 — Coaching clip: 1 call replay (good or bad), 2 min clip + 5 min discussion
- 0:42-0:45 — This week's individual commitments (each person states their target)

**Metrics reviewed:**
- Bookings, shows, closes, ACV, close rate by Closer, show rate by Setter
- Pipeline coverage (3x next 30 days minimum)
- Collection rate (target >95%)

**Decisions made here:**
- Week's script/offer — changes committed or killed
- Pipeline rebalancing — who gets the high-intent leads
- Training focus for the week

---

## 3. Marketing Weekly (Tue 10:00am, 45 min)

**Participants:** Marketing Mgr + Ad Buyer + Copywriter + Content Mgr (first 20 min)
**Agenda:**
- 0:00-0:05 — Numbers: leads, CAC, spend, MQL→SQL, attribution by source
- 0:05-0:15 — Paid channel review: Meta, Google, YouTube — spend, CPL, scale-or-kill decisions per creative
- 0:15-0:25 — Owned channels: email, organic, community — KPI + next week's plan
- 0:25-0:35 — Creative pipeline: what's in production, review queue, publishing slate
- 0:35-0:42 — Tests live and tests shipping — hypotheses + success criteria
- 0:42-0:45 — Asks of Operator / other departments

**Metrics reviewed:**
- CAC by channel + blended
- CPL by campaign, MER (Media Efficiency Ratio)
- Attributed revenue by source
- Creative win rate (hooks that held >3s, etc.)

**Decisions made here:**
- Kill/scale paid campaigns (>20% budget shift)
- New creative greenlit
- Test queue priority for the week

---

## 4. CS Weekly (Wed 11:00am, 45 min)

**Participants:** CS Mgr + Coaches + support staff + Ops Mgr (first 15 min)
**Agenda:**
- 0:00-0:05 — Numbers: NPS, churn this week, retention rate, active at-risk count
- 0:05-0:15 — At-risk client deep-dive: 3-5 names, root cause, intervention plan
- 0:15-0:25 — Onboarding pipeline: new clients this week, completion rate, drop-off points
- 0:25-0:35 — Client milestones hit + wins (feeds case studies)
- 0:35-0:42 — Product/offer feedback captured this week (fed back to Operator)
- 0:42-0:45 — Upsell / referral opportunities identified

**Metrics reviewed:**
- Churn rate (monthly, rolling)
- NPS (monthly sample)
- Time-to-first-win (days from signup to milestone 1)
- Milestone completion rate
- Refund rate

**Decisions made here:**
- Retention interventions for at-risk
- Program/curriculum changes (flag to Operator if material)
- Case study candidates handed to Content Mgr

---

## 5. Content Weekly (Thu 10:00am, 45 min)

**Participants:** Content Mgr + YouTube Producer + Video Editor + Copywriter + Operator (first 15 min)
**Agenda:**
- 0:00-0:05 — Numbers: pieces published, reach, engagement, subscribers added, attribution
- 0:05-0:15 — Content plan walk: next 2 weeks slate, any gaps
- 0:15-0:25 — Video operations: YouTube plan, short-form cadence, turnaround time
- 0:25-0:35 — Copy + email: sequences shipping, A/B tests, open/click rates
- 0:35-0:42 — Top performer review: 1 piece that overperformed — why? Replicate pattern.
- 0:42-0:45 — Asks / dependencies (creative briefs owed from other departments)

**Metrics reviewed:**
- Publishing cadence vs plan
- Platform-specific: YouTube CTR, AVD; IG Reels plays; TikTok completion rate
- Email: open >25%, CTR >2.5% benchmark
- Content → pipeline (attributed leads)

**Decisions made here:**
- Content plan changes (kill/add series)
- Production resource allocation
- Next month's content theme (flagged, decided at monthly content plan)

---

## 6. All-Hands (Fri 3:00pm, 30 min)

**Participants:** Full team
**Agenda:**
- 0:00-0:05 — Operator: numbers + 1 strategic message
- 0:05-0:15 — Department round: each lead gives 1 win + 1 learn (1 min each)
- 0:15-0:20 — Shoutouts: public recognition, peer-nominated
- 0:20-0:25 — 1 demo / Loom of cool work this week
- 0:25-0:30 — Questions → Operator (AMA, pre-submitted in Slack #all-hands)

**Rules:**
- Cameras on. All-hands is connective tissue; hiding kills it.
- No bad news buried here — deliver bad news in leadership or 1:1, not all-hands
- Recording posted in Notion for async team members and new hires

**Outputs:**
- Recording in Notion /all-hands archive
- Wins post in #wins channel
- Next week's 1 focus announced by Operator

## Metrics / Success Criteria
- All weekly cadences start on time 90%+ of weeks
- Pre-reads circulated 24h in advance 90%+ of weeks
- Meeting recaps posted within 2h 100% of weeks
- Leadership weekly makes ≥3 decisions per session
- All-hands attendance >90%
- Total leadership weekly load per Operator: ≤4 hrs/week

## Failure Modes
- **Weekly becomes status.** Fix: enforce "decisions made" section; cancel meetings with 0 decisions 2 weeks running.
- **Pre-read skipped, meeting re-briefs.** Fix: 5 min silent reading at top; rebrief-nobody-read is dead.
- **Department weekly owned by Operator.** Fix: named department heads drives; Operator is a participant.
- **All-hands goes negative.** Fix: bad news in leadership; all-hands is deliberately forward-looking.
- **Friday all-hands competes with end-of-week work.** Fix: hard-keep 3pm slot; protect it.
