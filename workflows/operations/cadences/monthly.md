# Monthly Cadences

## Purpose
The monthly rhythm is where the Operator and leadership step above the weekly grind to review the business, adjust plans, and set direction. Five standing monthly sessions. Total operator load: ~6-8 hours per month.

## Monthly Schedule (Standard Month)

| Timing | Cadence | Duration | Participants |
|---|---|---|---|
| Day 5 | Business Review (MBR) | 90 min | Operator + leadership + CFO |
| Day 7 | Content Retro | 60 min | Content + Marketing team |
| Day 7-10 | Launch Retro (if launch in prior month) | 60 min | Launch team + Operator |
| Day 20 | Next Month Content Plan | 90 min | Content + Marketing + Operator |
| Day 25 | Strategic Priorities Session | 60 min | Operator + direct reports |

"Day X" = business day X of the month. Shift ±2 days around holidays.

---

## 1. Monthly Business Review (MBR) — Day 5, 90 min

**Participants:** Operator, all direct reports, CFO (fractional counts)
**Pre-read (sent 48h in advance):**
- Dashboard snapshot (revenue, cash, leads, CAC, LTV, churn, NPS)
- CFO-produced P&L + commentary
- Department-level OKR/KPI progress

**Agenda:**
- 0:00-0:15 — CFO: financials (P&L, cash, runway, 13-week projection, variance to plan)
- 0:15-0:30 — Revenue + pipeline: Sales lead walks pipeline vs plan
- 0:30-0:45 — Acquisition: Marketing Mgr walks CAC, lead volume, channel performance
- 0:45-1:00 — Retention: CS Mgr walks churn, NPS, cohort retention
- 1:00-1:15 — Operational health: Ops Mgr walks team, hiring, SOP growth, tool spend
- 1:15-1:25 — 3 decisions needed from this review (pre-listed)
- 1:25-1:30 — Next month's one key focus (Operator announces)

**Key metrics (targets set at quarterly):**

| Metric | Owner | Target | This Month |
|---|---|---|---|
| Net new MRR | Sales | _ | _ |
| Cash in bank | CFO | >6 mo runway | _ |
| Blended CAC | Marketing | <1/3 of LTV | _ |
| Gross churn | CS | <5%/mo | _ |
| NPS | CS | >50 | _ |
| EBITDA margin | CFO | stage-dependent | _ |
| Headcount | Ops | per plan | _ |

**Outputs:**
- MBR deck archived in Notion `/business-reviews/`
- Decisions logged
- Next month's priority communicated in all-hands Friday

---

## 2. Content Retro — Day 7, 60 min

**Participants:** Content Mgr + YouTube Producer + Video Editor + Copywriter + Ad Buyer + Marketing Mgr
**Pre-read (sent 24h in advance):**
- Content performance spreadsheet: every piece published last month + views/CTR/engagement/attribution
- Top 3 performers + bottom 3 performers identified

**Agenda:**
- 0:00-0:10 — Numbers review: total reach, net subscribers, pipeline attribution
- 0:10-0:25 — Top 3 performers: why did they work? extractable patterns?
- 0:25-0:35 — Bottom 3 performers: what failed? stop doing, or fix format?
- 0:35-0:45 — Cadence audit: did we publish per plan? where did we slip? why?
- 0:45-0:55 — Pattern extraction: 1-2 patterns to codify (e.g., hook formats, thumbnail templates)
- 0:55-1:00 — Inputs for next month's content plan (handed to Day 20 session)

**Outputs:**
- Content retro doc in Notion `/retros/content/YYYY-MM/`
- Patterns added to `skills/hook-library/` and `skills/thumbnail-templates/`
- Kill list: formats or series ending this month

---

## 3. Launch Retro — Conditional, 60 min

Run only when a launch ran in the prior 30 days (cohort launch, product launch, campaign launch >$10k spend or >$25k revenue).

**Participants:** Launch team (Operator, Marketing Mgr, Sales lead, CS Mgr, Ad Buyer, Copywriter)
**Pre-read:**
- Launch results vs plan (revenue, leads, attendance, conversion)
- Spend vs budget
- Timeline slippage log

**Agenda:**
- 0:00-0:10 — Results vs plan (blunt numbers)
- 0:10-0:25 — What worked (keep for next launch)
- 0:25-0:40 — What didn't (stop or fix for next launch)
- 0:40-0:50 — Root causes of the top 3 issues (not symptoms)
- 0:50-0:55 — Launch playbook updates — who owns updating `frameworks/launch-playbook/`
- 0:55-1:00 — Actions + owners

**Rules:**
- Blameless format. Focus on systems, not people.
- No retro without data. If data isn't there, launch reporting is the first bug to fix.
- Playbook updates land within 7 days of retro.

**Outputs:**
- Launch retro doc in Notion `/retros/launches/YYYY-MM-launch-name/`
- Updated launch playbook
- Task list for the next launch's pre-mortem

---

## 4. Next Month Content Plan — Day 20, 90 min

**Participants:** Content Mgr (drives) + Marketing Mgr + YouTube Producer + Operator (first 30 min)
**Pre-read:**
- Prior month's content retro
- Upcoming offers / launches (from Marketing roadmap)
- Content theme for the month (set at quarterly planning)

**Agenda:**
- 0:00-0:15 — Theme + strategic goal (Operator frames, then drops off)
- 0:15-0:40 — Long-form slate: YouTube videos for next month (titles, hooks, guests, dates)
- 0:40-0:60 — Short-form slate: IG/TikTok/Shorts (volume, formats, pillars)
- 0:60-0:75 — Email + newsletter slate
- 0:75-0:85 — Dependency map: who needs what from whom by when
- 0:85-0:90 — Plan locked + published to Notion

**Outputs:**
- Content plan in Notion `/content-plans/YYYY-MM/`
- a productivity-software brand tasks created for every deliverable
- Creative briefs drafted for top 3 pieces (by end of week)

---

## 5. Strategic Priorities Session — Day 25, 60 min

**Participants:** Operator + all direct reports (Ops Mgr, Marketing Mgr, CS Mgr, Content Mgr, Sales lead)
**Purpose:** Look up from the month that just happened → is next month's plan still right? Adjust before month starts.

**Agenda:**
- 0:00-0:10 — Review quarterly OKRs — how are we tracking (from quarterly plan)
- 0:10-0:25 — Market / competitive signals seen this month (from `competitive-intel-ops.md`)
- 0:25-0:40 — Next month's top 3 priorities (one per major department, ideally)
- 0:40-0:55 — What we are stopping / deprioritizing to make room
- 0:55-1:00 — Operator locks the list, communicates in Friday all-hands

**Outputs:**
- Priorities doc in Notion `/priorities/YYYY-MM/`
- Top 3 priorities go into next MBR as a retro input
- Kill-list of things stopped

---

## Metrics / Success Criteria
- MBR happens on or before business day 5, 11/12 months
- CFO close lands by day 10, 11/12 months
- Content retro produces 1-2 codified patterns per month
- Launch retro shipped within 10 business days of launch end
- Content plan locked by day 25 for following month
- Strategic priorities doc ≤3 priorities (not 10)

## Failure Modes
- **MBR drifts to day 15.** Books aren't closing. Fix: CFO gets a hard deadline; day 5 MBR is fixed.
- **Retros without data.** Fix: reporting is a day-0 blocker; no retro without the spreadsheet.
- **Priorities list = 8+ items.** Fix: 3 max. If there are more, they aren't priorities.
- **Monthly content plan slides into the month.** Fix: day 20 is the gate; anything unplanned by day 1 gets dropped.
- **Retros become blameful.** Fix: name-free root cause analysis; owner is "process" not "person."
